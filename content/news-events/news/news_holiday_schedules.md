<div class="hwp-news-header__description">
<div class="hwp-mx-auto">
<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden">Summer is once again upon us, and with it, the annual change to staffing as our student staff graduates or goes home for the Summer. During this time, staffing may not be consistent, so we encourage all of our patrons to please email us at <a href="mailto:iqsslabs@harvard.edu">iqsslabs@harvard.edu</a> if they need help and are unable to find anyone at the helpdesk. We are available Monday - Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.</div>
