<div class="hwp-news-header__description">
<div class="hwp-mx-auto">
<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><h3>	 </h3><h3> <strong>Hardware:</strong></h3><p>	I am happy to announce that I have finished the deployment of a variety of new workstations in our labs.  These new workstations join our Optiplex 7090s from 2021 and the super-powered Mac Pro from 2019.</p><p>	The stations include the Dell Optiplex 7000, Apple Mac Studio Max and Ultra, the Dell Precision 7865 (coming soon) You can find more information about our new workstations on our <a href="/computer-labs/lab-workstation-specifications">workstations page.</a></p><h3> <strong>Software</strong></h3><p>	Upon the request of one of our patrons, we have recently received a lab-wide license for <a href="https://enscape3d.com/">Enscape 3D.</a> This software is now available on all of our Windows workstations, and we are preparing to release it on all macOS-based workstations in the coming weeks.</p><p>	We also received a request to install <a href="https://www.tableau.com/">Tableau</a> which has now been installed on all of our workstations.</p><p>	For a list of software available on these computers, please see our <a href="https://cls.iq.harvard.edu/lab-software">software page</a>. If you have any questions or concerns, please <a href="mailto:iqsslabs@harvard.edu">contact us.</a></p><h3> <strong>Item Checkouts</strong></h3><p>	Item checkouts are once again available. They are available Monday - Friday from 9 AM to 4:30 PM (Depending on staffing). All items must be returned by 4:30 PM on the same day.</p></div>
