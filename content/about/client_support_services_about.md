<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	We strive to provide prompt, high-quality service to the graduate students, staff, and faculty within the CGIS community. We are available to assist you Monday through Friday, between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM (EST).
