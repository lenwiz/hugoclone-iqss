## Can I eat in the lab?

Food is not permitted in the labs. Please stay hydrated but dispose of any food outside of the lab to keep the area free of pests that also love to consume wires.

## Can I print to lab printers from my personal computer?

Yes. You must select hmdc-crimson-print to be able to print to our printers. Instructions are available for both Windows and macOS laptops.

## May I remotely connect to lab workstations?

Yes, there are several workstations dedicated to remote access, but supplies are limited and available first come, first served. Email iqsslabs@harvard.edu for details.

## Can I use my own paper in the printer?

Yes, as long as the paper is verified to work with laser printers. Specialty paper (card stock, A3, 11x14, acid-free) is available behind the helpdesk.

## Can you help with GIS questions?

Lab staff are not trained in GIS. Please contact the CGA staff, whose office is directly in front of the elevators on the Concourse floor of CGIS Knafel. Visit [gis.harvard.edu](https://gis.harvard.edu) for more information.

## Does the lab sell or distribute software?

Software distribution stopped in 2015. Much of the installed software has free trials or academic licenses. FAS/GSAS affiliates should check the [FAS Downloads Page](https://downloads.fas.harvard.edu/).

## How long can I check out items for?

Items should normally be returned thirty minutes before the helpdesk closes on the same day. Extended checkouts can be arranged. Item checkouts are only available Monday-Friday, 9 AM to 4:30 PM. For permanent IT items, contact IQSS/HMDC Client Support Services.

## How much does it cost to print?

See our [printer information](../labs-printers/) page for pricing details.

## Where is the SB12 Training Room?

It is located in the sub-basement (SB), which can only be reached by elevator. After exiting the elevator, turn right and the room is located behind glass doors.

## Who can sign up for a lab account?

The labs are open to all active members of the Harvard and MIT Community.

## Why can't I login to lab computers?

A signup is required for all members. Please fill out the access request form. Access can only be granted Monday-Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.
