## How Does It Work?

1. **Submit your research** — Upload your paper, grant, or preregistration plan
2. **Select potential reviewers** — Choose from field-specific experts
3. **Quality peer review delivered in 30 days** — Receive constructive, detailed feedback
4. **Revise and submit for publication** — Strengthen your work before journal submission
