## Program Overview

IQSS invites Harvard social science tenure-track faculty to collaborate on research that challenges dominant scientific, political, or cultural assumptions. The program provides tailored support including high-level data expertise, infrastructure, and funding to help investigators collect the extraordinary evidence required to move the scientific community's dominant position the farthest distance.

## What is an "Extraordinary Claim"?

An extraordinary claim is an empirical claim that is unusually distant from the current dominant view of the scientific community.

### Examples include:

- Contrarian findings that challenge widely accepted assumptions
- Novel empirical insights into taboo, under-researched, or dismissed topics
- Investigations that expose structural flaws in prevalent methodologies
- Projects previously deemed "unfundable" or politically risky

## Confidentiality

The ECEE partnership is built on trust and an unusual level of confidentiality. All project proposals, data, and research discussions are treated as strictly confidential. Participants may disclose their involvement if they wish, but the ECEE team will never share anything without permission.

This high confidentiality level gives researchers the freedom to explore far riskier ideas than they would otherwise. Join our highly secure environment for candid, rigorous collaboration with our experts. By keeping ideas private, ECEE helps develop extraordinary evidence necessary to convince yourself and the world that you're right, and if not, to learn why.

## Eligibility

Harvard social science tenure-track faculty are eligible to apply.
