## IQSS Resources

- **Data Science Resources at IQSS** — Listing of Data Science resources available at IQSS
- **[rapidPeer](../../research-resources/rapidpeer/)** — Fast, constructive, double-blind peer review by a leading external scholar
- **[Extraordinary Claims, Extraordinary Evidence](../../research-resources/ecee/)** — Supercharged research support for projects with potential to move the needle
- **[Center for Geographic Analysis Training](https://gis.harvard.edu/teaching)** — GIS workshops
- **[Harvard Dataverse](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/)** — A platform for sharing, publishing, and archiving research data
- **[Program on Survey Research](https://psr.iq.harvard.edu/)** — Support with survey theory and practice

## Harvard Research Support

- **[FAS Research Computing](https://rc.fas.harvard.edu/)** — Computing cluster, support and training funded by FAS
- **[Research Computing Services, Harvard Business School](https://www.hbs.edu/research-computing-services/about-us/statistical-and-data-services.aspx)** — Support for statistical analysis and computing for HBS researchers
- **[Research Support Services, Harvard Graduate School of Education](http://its.gse.harvard.edu/services/research)** — Research software training (R, Stata, Mplus, NVivo, Atlas.ti) and consulting
- **[Digital Arts & Humanities at Harvard](https://digitalhumanities.fas.harvard.edu/)** — Support and consultation for digital humanities research
- **[Harvard Chan Bioinformatics Core](http://bioinformatics.sph.harvard.edu/)** — Consultations on research computing, bioinformatics and computational biology
- **[Copyright First Responders, Harvard Library](https://osc.hul.harvard.edu/programs/copyright/first-responders/)** — Have a copyright question? Ask the copyright first responders!
- **[Harvard Digital Scholarship Support Group](https://dssg.fas.harvard.edu/)** — Digital support for scholars
