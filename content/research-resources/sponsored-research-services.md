## IQSS Research Administration

The Sponsored Research Administration Team at IQSS is dedicated to supporting social scientists' research by delivering services across the full lifecycle of sponsored projects.

The team consists of experienced sponsored research administrators offering both pre-award and post-award support. They primarily serve social scientists in the Faculty of Arts and Sciences (FAS) and across the University, assisting with proposal development and submission, grants management, and reporting. Their goal is to provide outstanding service to IQSS-affiliated investigators with all of their sponsored research needs.

## Pre-Award Support — Proposal Support

Here is what we can do for you before your grant is awarded:

1. **Research and identify funding opportunities**
2. **Navigate funding opportunities** and provide application checklists
3. **Develop budgets, proposal documents, and application packages** with PIs and project team members
4. **Ensure accurate and complete proposals** compliant with non-federal/federal guidelines and university policies
5. **Coordinate with collaborators and administrators** internally and externally on projects and subcontracts
6. **Provide opportunities for peer review** of draft proposals prior to submission through the [rapidPeer service](../rapidpeer/)
7. **Shepherd proposals** through internal Harvard review and approval process through submission to sponsor

## Post-Award Support — Award Management

Once your grant is awarded, we support you through the entire project lifecycle:

1. **Act as liaison** to Harvard's Office for Sponsored Programs (OSP) on award negotiation and set up
2. **Provide pre-to-post award transition planning** and support on project activities including recruitment, effort allocations, start-up costs (e.g., computer hardware and software), subcontracts and vendor contracts
3. **Interpret university and sponsor guidelines** and advise PIs and project staff on matters of compliance and risk
4. **Provide monthly reconciliations and projections** to PIs on their sponsored projects
5. **Monitor expenses** and advise PIs and research staff on spending trends, personnel allocations, and expense allowability
6. **Prepare and negotiate vendor contracts**
7. **Process invoices and expense reimbursements**
8. **Assist with interim and annual progress reports** to the sponsor
9. **Manage the effort reporting process**

## How We Can Help

Whether you are developing a new proposal, managing an active grant, or navigating compliance questions, our team is here to help. We work with investigators at every stage:

- **New proposals** — We help identify funding opportunities and develop competitive applications
- **Active grants** — We provide ongoing financial management, reporting, and compliance support
- **Subcontracts** — We coordinate with collaborators at other institutions
- **Budget questions** — We advise on allowable expenses and help with projections
