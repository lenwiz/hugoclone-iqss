## IQSS Research Administration

The team provides pre- and post-award support primarily to Faculty of Arts and Sciences (FAS) researchers, but also across the University. Services include proposal development and submission, grants management, and reporting for IQSS-affiliated investigators.

## Pre-Award Support — Proposal Support

Here is what we can do for you:

1. Research and identify funding opportunities
2. Navigate funding opportunities and provide application checklists
3. Develop budgets, proposal documents, and application packages with PIs and project team members
4. Ensure accurate and complete proposals compliant with non-federal/federal guidelines and university policies
5. Coordinate with collaborators and administrators internally and externally on projects and subcontracts
6. Provide opportunities for peer review of draft proposals prior to submission
7. Shepherd proposals through internal Harvard review and approval process through submission to sponsor

## Post-Award Support — Award Management

1. Act as liaison to Harvard's Office for Sponsored Programs (OSP) on award negotiation and set up
2. Provide pre- to post-award transition planning and support (recruitment, effort allocations, start-up costs, subcontracts, vendor contracts)
3. Interpret university and sponsor guidelines; advise PIs and project staff on compliance and risk
4. Provide monthly reconciliations and projections to PIs on sponsored projects
5. Monitor expenses; advise PIs and research staff on spending trends, personnel allocations, and expense allowability
6. Prepare and negotiate vendor contracts
7. Process invoices and expense reimbursements
8. Assist with interim and annual progress reports to the sponsor
9. Manage the effort reporting process
