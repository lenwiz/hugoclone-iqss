## For Students: Get Experience

Interested undergraduates should visit the [Current Opportunities](/research-resources/undergraduate-research-scholars/#current-opportunities) section for projects needing researchers. To apply, students should send a resume and cover letter with relevant qualifications to the contact listed under the project description.

Even without listed opportunities, students can reach out directly to faculty whose research interests them to inform them about URS. Once a faculty member receives grant approval, they work with IQSS administrators to hire undergraduates.

### How It Works

1. Check the Research Opportunities for current projects
2. Send a resume and cover letter to the project contact
3. Alternatively, contact a faculty member directly about URS
4. Faculty obtains grant approval → IQSS hires the student

### Repeat Participation

Students may also participate in the program more than once, and are encouraged to apply in consecutive terms to continue or start new projects.

### Compensation & Hours

Students are paid **$18/hr** and are expected to spend between three to five hours per week at a minimum, but often more than this, working with their faculty sponsor and/or research group.

### Eligibility

- Must be a Harvard undergraduate
