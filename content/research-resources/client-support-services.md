## Common Support Requests

- **Network Drives** — Departmental file shares for staff to store and share files, with instructions for access on and off campus
- **Wifi Network Access** — All devices must be registered before connecting to the Harvard network
- **Printing** — Installation, troubleshooting, maintenance, and recycling of printers
- **Onboarding** — Help getting set up with your office system, phone, email, printer, and many other services on your first day
