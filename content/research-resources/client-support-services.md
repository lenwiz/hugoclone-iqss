## VPN Remote Access

To access your network space while off campus:

1. **Set up Okta:** Install and configure the Okta app on your smart device. Details at [key.harvard.edu](https://key.harvard.edu)
2. **Install Harvard's VPN Client:** Obtain the software from [vpn.harvard.edu](http://vpn.harvard.edu). Log in with your Harvard username, Key, and the Okta code. Download and install the Cisco VPN application.
3. **Connect:** When opening the app for the first time, the address to use is **vpn.harvard.edu/gencom**
4. **Authenticate:** Click Connect and enter your key information plus the Okta code.
5. **Acknowledge:** Click OK, then accept the prompt confirming the system is for Harvard associates only.
6. **Access resources:** Once connected, you can reach departmental shares and other Harvard resources.

## Printing Services

IQSS installs, troubleshoots, maintains, and recycles printers for all affiliates, supporting many brands and models.

**Key information:**

- Wireless networking of printers is not supported on the Harvard network
- Duplex printing (both sides) is available on most university printers — recommended to save paper
- HP brand printers are highly recommended for ease of use, longevity, and availability of consumables
- Remote printing to networked printers is possible via the FAS VPN
- IQSS does not supply printer toners for CGIS affiliates; contact your local purchasing agent

**Troubleshooting network printing:**

1. Power cycle the printer (off then on)
2. Reboot your computer
3. Check the printer for error messages
4. If unresolved, contact IQSS at help@iq.harvard.edu

## Network Drives

Departmental file shares provide a location for staff to store and share files. Access is available both on and off campus:

- **On campus:** Network drives are accessible directly when connected to the Harvard network
- **Off campus:** Connect via VPN first, then access network drives as usual
- All devices must be registered before connecting to the Harvard network

## Onboarding for New Employees

First day at work is always overwhelming, and we are here to help you get set up:

1. **Get your Harvard ID** at Smith Campus Center (Room 807, Phone: 617-495-8827)
2. **Claim your HarvardKey** and activate two-step verification — provides access to Harvard applications and services with a single login
3. **Email account** — Check with your department administrator or HR. Verify your O365 email via Outlook web application at office.com
4. **Voicemail setup** — Call 495-8168 and press #. Your ID number is the last 5 digits of your Harvard ID; temporary PIN is the full 7 digits
5. **Wi-Fi registration** — Connect to Harvard Secure Wi-Fi at getonline.harvard.edu. Also recommended: set up "eduroam" for use at other higher education institutions
