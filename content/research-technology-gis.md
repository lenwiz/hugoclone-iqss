## GIS Training Events

<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="/events/gis-institute-summer-2026/">GIS Institute Summer 2026</a></h3>
<p>A two-week training course in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and its application to research. Designed for Harvard graduate students, post-doctoral fellows, faculty, staff, and external researchers.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="/events/getting-started-ai-spatial-data/">Getting Started with AI for Spatial Data</a></h3>
<p>A 2-hour interactive workshop on practical use of Large Language Models (LLMs) for text classification using geotagged Twitter data with Python and LM Studio.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="/events/satellite-imagery-analysis-google-earth-engine/">Satellite Imagery Analysis with Google Earth Engine</a></h3>
<p>Two-hour workshop on satellite imagery analysis with GEE using KNIME's extension. Covers remote sensing for Social Science, Health, and Humanities research.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="/events/getting-started-spatial-analysis-r/">Getting Started with Spatial Analysis in R</a></h3>
<p>Two-hour workshop introducing spatial analysis concepts. Students use R to perform proximity, overlay, and geostatistical operations on various data.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="/events/getting-started-making-maps-qgis-and-arcgis/">Getting Started Making Maps with QGIS and ArcGIS</a></h3>
<p>Three-hour workshop on mapmaking using QGIS and ArcGIS Pro. Students create general reference maps and thematic maps.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="/events/visual-programming-gis-using-knime/">Visual Programming for GIS using KNIME</a></h3>
<p>Two-hour workshop on no-code visual programming for geographic analysis using KNIME. Covers reading, processing, visualizing, and writing GIS data.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="/events/geocoding-arcgis/">Geocoding with ArcGIS</a></h3>
<p>Two-hour workshop on geocoding concepts. Students geocode addresses and cities using Bing Maps, Google Maps, and ArcGIS Online.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="/events/introduction-gis/">Introduction to GIS</a></h3>
<p>Introductory workshop covering geographic file types, data formats, and GIS usage.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="/events/gis-humanities-and-social-sciences/">GIS for Humanities and Social Sciences</a></h3>
<p>Full-day hands-on workshop on GIS and mapping techniques for humanities and social sciences. Lead Instructor: Jeff Blossom.</p>
</div>
</div>
