# Welcome to the IQSS Archive

The Harvard Institute for Quantitative Social Science (IQSS) is a center of intellectual activity where researchers from across the social sciences come together to pursue research projects using quantitative methods. This site contains a comprehensive archive of IQSS content, including:

## Our Mission

IQSS brings together researchers from across the social sciences with the goal of improving research through:
- Methodological innovation
- Better research tools
- Collaborative research opportunities
- Data science services and support

## What You'll Find Here

### 📊 **Data Science Services**
Access to statistical consulting, data analysis resources, and computational support for your research.

Comprehensive profiles of over 1,000 IQSS faculty, staff, affiliates, and students.

### 🎓 **Programs & Research**
Information about our research programs, scientific initiatives, and collaborative projects.

### 📰 **News & Events**
