<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://socialscience.one/" target="_blank">Social Science One</a></h3>
<p>Implements a new partnership model between academics and the private sector to use company data for societal issues while maintaining privacy and data security standards.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://gis.harvard.edu/" target="_blank">The Center for Geographic Analysis</a></h3>
<p>Administers Harvard-wide GIS infrastructure, collects and disseminates spatial datasets, and provides training and consultation in the use of geospatial technologies.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://projects.iq.harvard.edu/futureofmedia" target="_blank">The Future of Media Project</a></h3>
<p>A partnership with HBS to further research and identify solutions to rebalance truth, privacy and power in the media industry.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://psr.iq.harvard.edu/" target="_blank">The Program on Survey Research</a></h3>
<p>Encourages and facilitates research and instruction in the theory and practice of survey research, with a mission of enhancing the quality of teaching and research at Harvard.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://hfh.fas.harvard.edu/" target="_blank">The Human Flourishing Program</a></h3>
<p>Aims to integrate knowledge from the empirical social sciences and the humanities on topics such as religion, virtue, work, purpose, forgiveness, marriage, and family.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://murray.harvard.edu/" target="_blank">The Henry A. Murray Research Archive</a></h3>
<p>A permanent repository for quantitative and qualitative social science data with over 100 TB of numerical datasets, video/audio recordings, interview notes, and other media.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://dlabss.harvard.edu/" target="_blank">DLABSS</a></h3>
<p>An online experiments and survey community for social science research connecting subject pool participants with researchers conducting surveys and experiments.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://projects.iq.harvard.edu/peblss" target="_blank">The Program on Experience Based Learning in the Social Sciences</a></h3>
<p>Provides a modern support infrastructure to Harvard programs and faculty that create active learning opportunities for Harvard students.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://projects.iq.harvard.edu/cbdb/" target="_blank">The China Biographical Database (CBDB)</a></h3>
<p>A freely accessible relational database with biographical information from Chinese historical records (7th–19th centuries) for statistical, social network, and spatial analysis.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="card-grid">
<div class="card">
<h3>Program on Higher Education Research</h3>
<p>Supports rigorous research on academic freedom and higher education. Home to the Council on Academic Freedom at Harvard, a group of 200+ Harvard faculty promoting civil discourse and viewpoint diversity.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3><a href="https://dataprivacylab.org/" target="_blank">The Data Privacy Lab</a></h3>
<p>Creates technologies and related policies that balance privacy protection and society's need to collect and share sensitive information for many worthy purposes.</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h3>The Privacy Insights Project</h3>
<p>Developing statistically valid differentially private systems, including OpenDP, that will also protect researchers and society.</p>
</div>
</div>
