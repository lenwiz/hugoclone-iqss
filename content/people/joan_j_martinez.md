<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Joan Martinez is a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of California Berkeley. She works on topics in labor, education economics, and applied econometrics. She received her MSc degree in Economics at University College London and BA at Universidad del Pacífico in Peru. She received a Chevening Award from the Foreign, Commonwealth, and Development Office (FCDO) from the United Kingdom. Prior to her doctoral studies, she worked as a research assistant at Universidad del Pacífico and an evaluation specialist in the Ministry of Economics and Finance.</p></div>
