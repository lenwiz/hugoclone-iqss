<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Federico Raimondi Slepoi is a behavioral economist. His research interests revolve around the understanding of heterogenous treatment effects in behavior change interventions. He is the founder and former managing director of R², the Department of Behavioural Policy of the Municipality of Rome and Italy’s first operative behavioural insights unit. He has lectured at the Sapienza University of Rome, the University of Milano-Bicocca, Erasmus University Rotterdam and Harvard University. Previously, he was the Behavioural Economics Adviser to the Italian Secretary of the Environment and a member of the Expert Behavioural Insights Network at the OECD and the United Nations International COVID-19 Behavioral Insights Working Group.</p></div>
