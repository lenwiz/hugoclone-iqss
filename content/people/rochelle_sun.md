<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Rochelle Sun is a PhD student in the Department of Government studying the role of healthcare in diplomacy and security. Her research seeks to understand how states and non-state actors strategically use mechanisms of healthcare to achieve political objectives, and under what circumstances these strategies are successful. She also supports research optimizing infectious disease care in conflict settings through her collaboration with GHESKIO, a healthcare and research center in Port-au-Prince, Haiti. She is a member of the associate board for the Alliance for International Medical Action (ALIMA). </p></div>
