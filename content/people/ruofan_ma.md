<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>Ruofan Ma is a PhD student in the Government Department at Harvard University. His research interests include international institutions, international trade, labor rights, and human rights. In particular, his works focus on the effects of international organizations and multinational firms on domestic labor standards. Before starting his PhD, Ruofan completed a BA in Politics at Peking University and an AM in East Asia Studies at Harvard.</p></div>
