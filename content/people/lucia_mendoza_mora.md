<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Lucía Mendoza is a PhD student in the Department of Government. She studies the political economy of democratic accountability, corruption, and political violence in Latin America. She holds BAs in Economics and History, and an MA in Economics from Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia. Her prior research experience focused on civil war, crime, and police-community relations.</p></div>
