Juan Pablo (JP) Chauvin is a PhD candidate in Public Policy. His research focuses on Development EcoNomics, Urban EcoNomics and Labor EcoNomics, with an emphasis on the role of gender, human capital and productive structures in the development of cities and regions; particularly in low and middle-income countries.
