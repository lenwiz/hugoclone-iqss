# Wei

Wei-Fang Hsieh

email

weifanghsieh@g. harvard. edu

Wei-Fang is a third year PhD student in the Department of Linguistics at Harvard. Her main research areas are syntax, semantics, the syntax-semantics interface and laboratory phonology. Her research interests include the syntax and semantics of reduplication, ellipsis and resultatives, and the phonetics and phonology of tone sandhi.
