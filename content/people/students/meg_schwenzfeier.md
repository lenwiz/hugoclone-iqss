<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><br/>Meg Schwenzfeier is a PhD student in Government at Harvard University. Her substantive research interests focus on survey methodology, public opinion, and political campaigns. She is currently working on projects using text analysis to measure racial attitudes and exploring survey non-response bias using voter files. Prior to arriving at Harvard she worked in political analytics, doing polling and data science for progressive organizations and campaigns.</div>
