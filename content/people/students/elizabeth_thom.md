# Elizabeth Thom

email

Elizabeth Thom is a PhD candidate in Government and Social Policy. Her research focuses on the politics of the American welfare state, federalism, political economy and political geography. She utilizes quantitative methods to map the spatial dimensions of welfare state dependence and political attitudes.
