# Nefara Riesch

Nefara Riesch

email

nriesch@g.harvard.edu

Nefara Riesch is a PhD student in Sociology and Social Policy. Her work examines the determinants and consequences of policing and criminal legal system policy change. Prior to doctoral studies, Nefara worked in policy research, focusing on homelessness, housing insecurity, and policing. Nefara holds an MPP from the University of California, Berkeley and a BA in History from UCLA.
