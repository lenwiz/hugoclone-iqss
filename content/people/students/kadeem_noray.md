# Kadeem Noray

Kadeem Noray

email

knoray@g. harvard. edu

Kadeem Noray is a PhD student in Public Policy at Harvard University. His research interests include labor ecoNomics, public ecoNomics, and applied ecoNometrics. He is currently working on estimating the causal effect of police brutality on local crime rates in Chicago and analyzing the long term impacts of various early 20th century U. S. public health policies.
