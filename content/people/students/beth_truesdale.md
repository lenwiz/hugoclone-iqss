<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden">Beth Truesdale is a doctoral student in Sociology and a doctoral fellow in Inequality and Social Policy at the Harvard Kennedy School. Her work has also been supported by Harvard's Center on the Developing Child, the Center for American Political Studies (CAPS), the Weatherhead Center, and the Tobin Project. Beth's current research examines the relationship between evidence and early childhood policy in the US and UK. Other research interests include inequality, education, and labor markets.</div>
