<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Juan Pablo Tosca is a talented software developer who joined IQSS in 2023 as a Senior Software Developer on the Dataverse project. Before joining IQSS, Juan worked at the Harvard Graduate School of Education as a Senior Software Developer specializing in Java and Spring applications. </p><p>	He is passionate about his work and started getting involved with the Dataverse and the Containerization Working Group a few months before joining the team. He is thrilled to contribute and be part of the Dataverse releases since 6.1.</p><p>	Originally from the town of Xochimilco (which means “place of the flowers” in Nahuatl,) located in Mexico City, Juan arrived in the United States in 2014 after finishing his MBA at Universidad del Valle de Mexico. Juan is fluent in both English and Spanish.</p><p>	In his free time, Juan loves to talk about immigration, music, and technology.</p><p>	 </p></div>
