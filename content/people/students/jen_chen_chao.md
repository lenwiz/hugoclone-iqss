# Jen

Jen-Chen Chao

email

jenchenchao@fas. harvard. edu

Jen-Chen Chao is a PhD student in Sociology. He studies how social inequality persists within and across generations, with a particular interest in the roles of educational institutions and family structure. He is also interested in developing and applying causal inference techniques to uncover potential policies that equalize opportunities.
