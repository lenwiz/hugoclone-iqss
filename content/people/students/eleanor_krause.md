# Eleanor Krause

Eleanor Krause

email

eleanorkrause@fas. harvard. edu

Eleanor is a PhD candidate in Public Policy at the Harvard Kennedy School. Her research considers the intersection of place, environment, and economic inequality. She is particularly interested in understanding how the characteristics of local labor markets determine how individuals and communities adjust to economic and environmental change.
