# Anny Tazio Hal Rubinson Fenton

Anny Tazio Hal Rubinson Fenton

email

afenton@fas. harvrd. edu

Anny is a doctoral candidate in Sociology and a doctoral fellow in Inequality and Social Policy at the Harvard Kennedy School. She is a multi-method researcher and her dissertation combines ethNography in pediatric offices, interviews, and pediatric patient visit recordings to investigate why HPV vaccination rates are so low in the US.
