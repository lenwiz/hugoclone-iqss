# Babak Fotouhi

Babak Fotouhi

Babak employs concepts from sociology, behavioral economics, and network science to study human behavior and interaction theoretically and experimentally. Broadly, he studies the linkages and mutual influences of two categories of entities. The first one subsumes social structure, social context, and culture.
