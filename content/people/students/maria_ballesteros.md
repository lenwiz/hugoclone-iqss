<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>			Maria Ballesteros is a PhD Student in Government at Harvard University using quantitative methods to study the role of gender in political violence. Originally from Mexico City, María graduated with honors from ITAM with a B.A. in Political Science. Prior to coming to Harvard, María worked as a research assistant at the Harris School of Public Policy, at The University of Chicago, researching the political economy of conflicts in Colombia, Chile and Afghanistan.</p></div>
