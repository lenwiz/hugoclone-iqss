Omar Barbiero is a Ph. D. candidate in Economics at Harvard University. He uses both empirical and theoretical methods to study the economic effects of exchange rate exposures, fiscal policies, and trade policies. His current research explores how foreign-priced operations generate currency mismatch exposure on balance sheet of firms.
