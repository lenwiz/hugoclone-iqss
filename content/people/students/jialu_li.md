# Jialu Li

Jialu Li

email

jialu_li@g.harvard.edu

Jialu Li is a PhD student in the Department of Government at Harvard University. Her research focuses on comparative political economy, political development, and state-business relationship, especially around how states interact with foreign firms. Jialu holds a B.A. in Political Science from Peking University and an M.A. in Regional Studies–East Asia from Harvard University.
