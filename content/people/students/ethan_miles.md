# Ethan Miles

Ethan Miles

email

ethanmiles@g.harvard.edu

Ethan Miles is a PhD Candidate in the Department of Government. He is broadly interested in International Political Economy, with a focus on the politics of global production, corporate lobbying behavior, and regulatory politics. His current research agenda deals with perceptions of firm "foreignness" by governments and mass publics, and how foreign firms influence domestic policymaking.
