# Annika Khandelwal

Annika Khandelwal

Annika is a sophomore at Harvard College studying Women, Gender, and Sexuality. She is interested in second and third wave feminist activism, as well as queer theory. At IQSS, she researches equitable transitions to renewable energy in low income communities on the Gulf Coast.
