<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>			Zhongyu Wei is a PhD student in the Human Development, Learning and Teaching program at Harvard University. She is interested in exploring how young children's curiosity and inquiry drive their language and cognitive development. She uses quantitative and computational methods to analyze observational data. Zhongyu holds a B.A. in linguistics and economics from Tsinghua University and an EdM in international education policy from the Harvard Graduate School of Education.</p></div>
