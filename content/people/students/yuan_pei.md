# Yuan Pei

Yuan Pei

email

yuanpei@g. harvard. edu

Yuan Pei is a doctoral student in Public Policy at Harvard. Yuan's research focuses on development economics and behavioral economics. His current research explores how changing social norms can affect people's decision to enter the civil service. Another project of his is studying the impact of distance learning on reducing urban-rural education gap in China.
