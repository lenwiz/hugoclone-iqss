<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden">Priya is a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Economics. She is broadly interested in labor ecoNomics and the ecoNomics of social networks. Current projects include the heuristic basis of decision fatigue using data from the New Hampshire prison parole system, the role of local natural resource booms and busts on educational attainment behavior, and the mechanism of peer effects in medical treatment settings. Priya earned her B.A. in Economics from Dartmouth College in 2013.</div>
