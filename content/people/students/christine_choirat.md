# Christine Choirat

Christine Choirat

I am a Senior Research Scientist at the Harvard T. H. Chan School of Public Health and at the Institute for Quantitative Social Science. I was trained as a statistician (PhD 2003, Paris Dauphine). My research interests are data science and high-performance computing, reproducible research, and environmental policy and health policy.
