# Caterina Chiopris

Caterina Chiopris

Caterina is a PhD candidate in Political Economy and Government. Her research focuses on regional inequalities in innovation, technology, and state building. Methodologically, she is interested in applied text analysis and natural experiments. Before joining Harvard, she earned a master's degree in Analytical Political Economy from Duke University.
