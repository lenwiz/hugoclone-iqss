# Sahil Chinoy

Sahil  Chinoy

email

Sahil Chinoy is a PhD student in economics. He studies political economy, U.S. history, and applied statistics, using econometric methods to explore questions about partisanship, political geography, and intergenerational mobility. Previously, he was a graphics editor at The New York Times. Before that, he studied physics and economics at UC Berkeley.
