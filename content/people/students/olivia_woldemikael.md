# Olivia Woldemikael

Olivia Woldemikael

email

woldemikael@g.harvard.edu

Olivia Woldenmikael's research focuses on the attitudes of host communities towards refugees and immigrants in the Global South. Her dissertation project aims to identify the determinants of individual-level attitudes, explaining why certain individuals and communities are receptive to forced migrants and others are hostile.
