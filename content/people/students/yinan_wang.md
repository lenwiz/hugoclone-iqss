# Yinan Wang

Yinan Wang

email

Yinan is a doctoral student in Sociology at Harvard University. Her research interests broadly focus on exploring how social institutions interact to create inequalities and stratifications along axes of gender, class, and race in the labor market using experiment methods. She received her BA in English and Sociology from Tsinghua University.
