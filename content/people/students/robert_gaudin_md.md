# Robert Gaudin, MD

Robert Gaudin, MD

email

robert-andre. gaudin@charite. de

Doctor Gaudin graduated from Medical School, Hamburg, Germany and defended his doctoral thesis in 2014. From January 2015 until June 2016 he was a research fellow at Harvard Medical School, Department of Facial Plastics MGH/MEEI working on the development of a Bionic Face in an animal model.
