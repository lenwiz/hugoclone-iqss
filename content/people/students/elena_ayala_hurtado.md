# Elena Ayala

Elena Ayala-Hurtado

email

Elena Ayala-Hurtado's research lies at the intersection of cultural sociology and the sociology of work. Her cross-national research explores how educationally advantaged young adults understand themselves, their trajectories, and their job-seeking strategies in contexts of increasing insecurity.
