<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>			Ohjae Gowen is a doctoral student in Sociology at Harvard University. He studies causes and consequences of childbearing, with a particular focus on gendered labor market dynamics. He is interested in applying quantitative methods to analyzing longitudinal data. Before coming to Harvard, Ohjae received his BA and MA in Sociology from Seoul National University, South Korea. He is the recipient of the scholarship funded by the Korea Foundation for Advanced Studies.</p></div>
