Aljoša Milenković is a PhD candidate in Linguistics at Harvard University with research interests in phonology, historical linguistics, metrics, syntax, computational and corpus linguistics. Aljoša uses corpora and experimental methods to study natural language sound patterns, sentence structure, and poetic meter.
