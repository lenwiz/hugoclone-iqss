# Roman Klimke

Roman Klimke

email

klimke@fas. harvard. edu

Roman Klimke is a Ph. D. candidate in Public Policy at Harvard University. His research interests lie at the intersection of labor and public economics. His current research investigates how labor markets respond to the introduction of social programs, for which he uses individual- and establishment-level Social Security data from Germany and Saudi Arabia.
