# Roza Nalbandyan

Roza Nalbandyan

Roza Nalbandyan is a Postdoctoral Fellow with the Human Flourishing Program at Harvard University. Her research develops and applies advanced statistical and measurement models — including psychometrics, machine learning, and computational methods — to examine measurement comparability, validity, student development, and academic flourishing.
