# Mary Laski

Mary Laski

email

marylaski@g. harvard. edu

Mary Laski is a PhD candidate in Education at Harvard University and a PIER Fellow at the Center for Education Policy Research. She studies education policy and the economics of education, with a particular focus on teacher quality, teacher labor markets, and the principal's role in school improvement.
