<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Dave is the co-founder of 99 Capital, a venture fund investing in frontier technology and markets. Before that, he led technical investments across blockchain &amp; fintech for SoftBank's $5B Latin America Fund. He has deep research interests in AI / ML applications particularly in binary outcome events like survey markets, where he is working with Prof. Chase Harrison on research at the IQSS in this area. Dave earned his BA in Statistics from Harvard, Cum Laude with a thesis on AI / ML applications on predictive survey methodology.</p></div>
