# Dorothy Manevich

Dorothy Manevich

email

dmanevich@g. harvard. edu

Dorothy is a PhD candidate in the Department of Government interested in political psychology and political behavior. Her work employs both quantitative and qualitative methods, with a particular emphasis on experiments and surveys. Dorothy’s dissertation investigates the psychological and social roots of political activism.
