<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden">Mikko Silliman is a Ph.D. student in Education Policy &amp; Program Evaluation interested in issues at the intersection of education policy and labor economics - particularly as they relate to inequality. His research focuses on applying quasi-experimental designs to learn from administrative data sets. Before beginning his doctoral studies, he conducted research at the VATT Institute for Economic Research and the OECD. He holds a MSSc in Social &amp; Public Policy from the University of Helsinki, a BA in Economics and in Philosophy, summa cum laude, from Tufts University, and was a Visiting Student at the University of Oxford. </div>
