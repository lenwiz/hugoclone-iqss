<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Don Sizemore became involved with IQSS through the Dataverse project when he joined the Odum Institute in 2015. He is the primary author of the Dataverse-Ansible role, a consultant through the Global Dataverse Community Consortium, and attempts to support the Dataverse community over its various communication channels. He holds a Master of Information Science from UNC's School of Information and Library Science, but highly cherishes his undergraduate minor in music performance on pipe organ.</p></div>
