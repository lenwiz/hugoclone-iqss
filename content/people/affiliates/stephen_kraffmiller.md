<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Stephen has been a devloper on the Dataverse Project since 2010. He is involved in all aspects of the Dataverse project, from the front end UI development to the back end business logic. For the Dataverse project he was the lead developer for custom dataset metadata fields, persistent identifiers, guestbook and download tracking.  He also implemented dataset templates and external query tools. He has given three presentations at JavaOne conferences over the years including "jQuery Integration with JSF 2 Libraries" with Mike Heppler and "Custom Bean Validation" with Gustavo Durand.  Currently he is spearheading the effort to make Dataverse data available via the Google Data Commons. In his spare time he enjoys running, reading, cooking and following the career of a talented playwright who happens to be his daughter.</p><p>	 </p></div>
