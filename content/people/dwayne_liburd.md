<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Dwayne Liburd is the Data Technology Support Associate in Data Acquisition and Archiving. His responsibilities include assisting the Manager of Data Acquisition, providing assistance on using Dataverse services, and providing user support for Dataverse. Dwayne also maintains the Henry A. Murray Research Archive Dataverse. He performs the cataloging of the metadata, storage of data related to the archive, and data processing, preservation, and dissemination of the original holdings of the Archive.</p><p>	 </p></div>
