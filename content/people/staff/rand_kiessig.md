# Rand Kiessig

**Email:** rkiessig@iq.harvard.edu

## Biography

Rand Kiessig     


Manager, IQSS Computer Lab Services









 







 
email
rkiessig@iq.harvard.edu






Rand Kiessig joined IQSS in May 2012 and has led IQSS Computer Lab Services since 2015. A California native, Rand has lived across the U.S. — from Washington State to North Carolina to Massachusetts — with stints in Mexico and Spain along the way. Before coming to Harvard, he held a range of technical roles at organizations including Microsoft, Verio, Data General, and Iron Mountain.Recommended Apps:1Password: Having a password manager is extremely important, and 1Password will help you do that while also alerting you to password that may be compromised.
