<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Gustavo Durand is the Technical Lead and Architect of Dataverse, an open source data repository platform developed by IQSS at Harvard University. Gustavo has been on Dataverse in many roles since its inception and in his current role, leads the architecture and technical design of the platform, reviews code from internal and and external developers, assists the Dataverse Community contributors, and overall works closely with the Development Project Manager. He began his Java programming career at Cambridge Technology Partners in 1997 and has over 20 total years experience as a software developer and consultant. <br/> </p></div>
