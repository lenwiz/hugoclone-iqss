# Kathryn Davidson

Kathryn Davidson

email

kathryndavidson@fas. harvard. edu

I'm an assistant professor in Linguistics at Harvard University where my research investigates the unique capacity that we have to understand an infinite number of sentences that we've never heard before (semantics), how we incorporate contextual information into these meanings (pragmatics), and how we ever learn to do this (development).
