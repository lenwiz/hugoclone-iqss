# Matthew Stephenson

Matthew Stephenson

Matthew Stephenson is Eli Goldston Professor of Law at Harvard Law School, where he teaches administrative law, legislation and regulation, and political economy of public law. His research focuses on the application of positive political theory to public law, particularly in the areas of administrative procedure, judicial institutions, and separation of powers.
