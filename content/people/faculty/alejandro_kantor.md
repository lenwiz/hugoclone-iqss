# Alejandro Kantor

Alejandro Kantor

Alejandro is an IQSS Fellow working on research related to course data from HarvardX and the EdX platform with Professor Dustin Tingley and the Office of the Vice Provost for the Advancement in Learning. Through his research, Alejandro develops statistical models as well as interactive visualizations.
