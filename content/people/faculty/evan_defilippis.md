<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	My research lies at the intersection of behavioral economics and psychology, applying formal modeling techniques to understand complex social systems. My research focuses on three main topics:</p><ol> <li>		How to optimize organizational and project design principles to enhance creative ideation, crowdsourcing, and collaborative outcomes.	</li> <li>		Behavioral interventions to increase the likelihood of conflict resolution	</li> <li>		The psychology of persuasion and negotiation	</li></ol><p>	Through my affiliation with IQSS, I am implementing a theoretically-grounded intervention designed to reduce affective polarization between political partisans and quantifiably increase receptivity to cross-ideological persuasion.</p></div>
