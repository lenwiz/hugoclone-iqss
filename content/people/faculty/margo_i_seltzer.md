# Margo I. Seltzer

Margo I. Seltzer

email

margo@eecs. harvard. edu

Margo Seltzer is the Herchel Smith Professor of Computer Science and a Harvard College Professor in the Harvard's School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. She received an A. B. degree in Applied Mathematics from Harvard/Radcliffe College in 1983 and a Ph. D. in Computer Science from the University of California, Berkeley, in 1992.
