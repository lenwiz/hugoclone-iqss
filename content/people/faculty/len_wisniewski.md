# Len Wisniewski

Len Wisniewski

email

lwisniewski@iq. harvard. edu

Len Wisniewski is Senior Director of Research Computing at IQSS. Before IQSS, Len was Engineering Manager for the High Performance Computing group at Sun Microsystems, leading the teams developing the Sun HPC ClusterTools and Sun Studio Performance Analyzer products. In that role, he actively supported the Open MPI open-source community.
