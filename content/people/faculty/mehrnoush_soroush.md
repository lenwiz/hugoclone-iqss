# Mehrnoush Soroush

Mehrnoush Soroush

Mehrnoush Soroush is a landscape archaeologist specializing in ancient water management systems in the Middle East. She holds a Ph. D. in the Study of the Ancient World, from New York University an MA in Architecture from the University of Tehran, Iran.
