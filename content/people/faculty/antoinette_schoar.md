# Antoinette Schoar

Antoinette Schoar

Antoinette Schoar is the Michael M. Koerner Associate Professor of Entrepreneurial Finance at the MIT Sloan School of Management. She has been teaching at Sloan for six years in the areas of corporate finance and entrepreneurship. While starting at Sloan she has developed a new course on Entrepreneurial finance which is a second year elective.
