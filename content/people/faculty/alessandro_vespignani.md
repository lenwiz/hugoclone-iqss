# Alessandro Vespignani

Alessandro Vespignani

Alessandro Vespignani is Sternberg Distinguished Professor at Northeastern University with appointments  in the College of Computer and Information Sciences, the Bouve' College of Health Sciences and the Department of Physics. Prof. Vespignani received his undergraduate degree and Ph. D.
