# Han Nguyen

Han Nguyen

Han is a first-year student at Harvard College, pursuing a concentration in Government and Economics. Her academic interests lie in exploring the intersections of foreign policy, public health, and international development. Currently, Han is working with Professor Pia Raffler on a project aimed at understanding power dynamics between bureaucrats and local politicians in developing nations.
