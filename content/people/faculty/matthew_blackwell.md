Matthew Blackwell is an Associate Professor of Government at Harvard University. He studies political methodology, with a focus on dynamic causal inference, instrumental variables, experimental design, missing data, and panel data.  His substantive interests include the lasting effects of slavery on political attitudes in the US South.
