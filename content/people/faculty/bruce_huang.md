Dr. Bruce Huang is currently the director of master's degree program in information technology, overseeing five Master of Liberal Arts (ALM) in Extension Studies degree programs, including computer science, cybersecurity, data science, digital media design, and systems engineering. Dr. Huang is a scholar-practitioner in the field of computer science.
