# Christina J Cross

Christina J Cross

Christina J. Cross is an Assistant Professor of Sociology at Harvard. Her research falls at the intersection of families, race/ethnicity, and social inequality. Using a variety of quantitative methods, she examines how the family, one of the oldest social institutions, shapes individuals’ life chances.
