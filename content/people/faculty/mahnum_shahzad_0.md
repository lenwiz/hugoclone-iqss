Mahnum Shahzad is an Instructor at the Department of Population Medicine at Harvard Medical School and the Harvard Pilgrim Healthcare Institute. Her work focuses on US pharmaceutical policy with a focus on how regulation impacts which drugs patients eventually receive, and what we know about those products.
