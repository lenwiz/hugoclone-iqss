# Mina Cikara

Mina Cikara

Lab Website

Professor Cikara studies how the mind, brain, and behavior change when the social context shifts from “me and you” to “us and them. ” She focuses primarily on how group membership, competition, and prejudice disrupt the processes that allow people to see others as human and to empathize with others.
