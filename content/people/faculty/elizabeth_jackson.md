# Elizabeth Jackson

Elizabeth Jackson

email

ejackson@iq. harvard. edu

Elizabeth Jackson is the Executive Director of IQSS. Elizabeth joined IQSS in July 2022 from both Greater Portland Health in Maine, and the Department of Global Health and Population at the Harvard TH Chan School of Public Health. Elizabeth has dedicated her career to work in alignment with the mission of IQSS.
