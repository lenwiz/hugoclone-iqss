# Roland Pongou

Roland Pongou is a Visiting Scientist in the Department of Global Health and Population at Harvard and an Affiliate of the Harvard Institute for Quantitative Social Science. He is an Associate Professor of Economics at the University of Ottawa in Canada, and an affiliated faculty member of the African School of Economics in Benin.
