Glenn is the Director of Engineering for VPAL (Vice Provost for Advances in Learning) at Harvard. Since 2014, Glenn has developed multi-cloud platform software to address the challenge of connecting teaching and learning data from de-centralized systems across Harvard in a secure, flexible, cost-effective and scalable way.
