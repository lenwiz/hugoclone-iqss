<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Aljoša Milenković is a PhD candidate in Linguistics at Harvard University with research interests in phonology, historical linguistics, metrics, syntax, computational and corpus linguistics. Aljoša uses corpora and experimental methods to study natural language sound patterns, sentence structure, and poetic meter. His ongoing projects combine Maximum Entropy models with machine learning techniques to analyze and model phonological variation.</p></div>
