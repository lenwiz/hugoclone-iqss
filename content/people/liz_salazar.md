<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Liz Salazar has been at IQSS since 2010, meandering into her current role through management of the IQSS website and social media channels. As Communications Coordinator, she oversees the Institute's online presence, publicity, and external communications, as well as updates to the IQSS affiliate database. She came to IQSS from the Harvard Foundation (part of Harvard College Student Life), and before coming to Harvard she worked at the Center for English Language at Boston University.</p><p>	Liz holds a BA in art history (with a minor in myth studies) from Boston University, and an ALM in museum studies from the Harvard Extension School. Insomniacs are invited to ask about her theater-related hobbies. </p><p>	 </p></div>
