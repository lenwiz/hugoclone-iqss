<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Dr. Pop currently serves as  Director in the Laboratory for Innovation Science at Harvard (LISH) l at the D^3 Institute within Harvard Business School. In this capacity, alongside Prof Karim Lakhani and a team of investigators, researchers and collaborators, Dr. Pop is overseeing and contributing to the ongoing scientific projects and research initiatives within the laboratory and D^3.  Her efforts are dedicated to supporting scientific and research activities that specifically address the integration and impact of artificial intelligence in business and society.  Prior to assuming the role at LISH/D^3, Dr. Pop conducted research at FAS within two stem cell laboratories. Notably, in Doug Melton lab at Harvard, she performed research aimed at developing stem cell therapies for Type 1 diabetes. Dr. Pop brings a wealth of experience to her current position, boasting two decades of research expertise in natural sciences.</p></div>
