After 15 years at Harvard as Chief Data Science and Technology Officer at IQSS, Merce Crosas departed to serve as Secretary of Open Government in Catalonia, Spain. Her departure marked the end of an era in which she helped build IQSS's data science infrastructure from the ground up.

Crosas led the development of the Dataverse Project, growing it from a Harvard-centric tool to a globally adopted research data platform with over 100 installations worldwide. Under her leadership, the project became a model for how academic institutions can create sustainable open-source infrastructure that serves the broader research community.

Her appointment as Secretary of Open Government represents a natural evolution of her career-long commitment to making data open and accessible. Crosas's legacy at IQSS—including Dataverse, data curation best practices, and a culture of open science—continues to influence how researchers worldwide share and preserve their work.
