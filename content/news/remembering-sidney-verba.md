A tribute to Sidney Verba of Harvard, who passed on March 4, 2019. Verba was a towering figure in political science whose work on political participation, civic engagement, and democratic theory influenced generations of scholars. He was also a founding influence on what would become IQSS.

Verba's research, particularly his landmark study "Voice and Equality" (with Kay Lehman Schlozman and Henry Brady), established the modern framework for understanding why some citizens participate in politics while others do not. His emphasis on rigorous empirical measurement of democratic engagement exemplified the quantitative approach that IQSS champions.

Beyond his scholarly contributions, Verba was renowned as a mentor, colleague, and institution builder. His legacy at Harvard includes not only his research but the collaborative culture he fostered—a spirit that lives on at IQSS, where the cloud research software "Sid" was named in his honor.
