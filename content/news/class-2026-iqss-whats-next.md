This May 2026 article celebrates IQSS-affiliated graduate students completing their Harvard programs and shares their next career steps. The graduating affiliates are heading to prestigious positions across academia and beyond. Dorothy Manevich joins the UAWs national political department. Jeremiah Cha and Andrew ODonoghue are both heading to Princeton. Raul Duarte will do a postdoc at Yales Cowles Foundation before joining Florida International University. Additional graduates include David Beavers joining Wake Forest, Ruofan Ma doing a postdoc at MIT, Kiara Hernandez heading to Columbias Center for Political Economy, and Daniel Lowery joining Yale.
