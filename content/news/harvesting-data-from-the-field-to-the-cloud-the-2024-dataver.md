The 10th anniversary Dataverse Community Meeting was held March 4-8 at CIMMYT (International Maize and Wheat Improvement Center) in Texcoco, Mexico. The location reflected Dataverse's growing importance in agricultural and development research.

The meeting focused on collaboration and the future of data systems, bringing together Dataverse users and developers from institutions around the world. Sessions covered topics from repository administration to data curation best practices, with particular attention to the needs of researchers in the Global South.

The anniversary milestone provided an opportunity to reflect on Dataverse's growth from a Harvard-centric project to a truly global research infrastructure, while the Mexico venue symbolized the project's commitment to serving diverse research communities regardless of geographic or economic barriers.
