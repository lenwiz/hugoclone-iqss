A celebration of Jeffry Frieden—a die-hard Yankees fan—and his 30 years at Harvard. Frieden, an IQSS faculty affiliate and Stanfield Professor of International Peace, has been a leading scholar of international political economy throughout his distinguished career.

Frieden's research examines how economic interests shape political outcomes, with particular focus on exchange rate politics, financial crises, and the political economy of European integration. His work combines rigorous quantitative analysis with deep historical knowledge, exemplifying the kind of interdisciplinary scholarship IQSS supports.

The lighthearted headline belies a serious academic legacy: Frieden has trained generations of political economists, published influential books and articles, and helped establish international political economy as a central subfield of political science.
