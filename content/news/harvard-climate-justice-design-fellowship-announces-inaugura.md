The Harvard Climate Justice Design Fellowship selected seven inaugural fellows who are leaders in environmental justice advocacy, organizing, and law from across the United States. The program, launched by IQSS, supports climate justice work through data science and design.

Fellows participate in intensive training on data analysis, visualization, and design thinking, then apply these skills to climate justice challenges in their home communities. The program bridges the gap between technical capability and grassroots organizing, ensuring that data tools serve community needs.

This initiative represents IQSS's commitment to applying quantitative social science methods to pressing societal challenges. By embedding data science expertise within climate justice movements, the fellowship aims to strengthen the evidence base for policy advocacy and community-led solutions.
