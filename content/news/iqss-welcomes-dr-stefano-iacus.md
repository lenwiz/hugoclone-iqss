The Institute for Quantitative Social Science is delighted to welcome Dr. Stefano Iacus as Senior Research Scientist, bringing extensive expertise in computational statistics, time series analysis, and statistical software development to IQSS's research and methodological innovation initiatives. Dr. Iacus's arrival strengthens IQSS's capacity to support cutting-edge quantitative research and advance the development of statistical tools used by scholars worldwide.

Dr. Iacus joins IQSS with an impressive background spanning academic research, open-source software development, and practical applications of statistical methods to real-world problems. His expertise in simulation-based inference, computational methods, and statistical software has contributed to major research computing projects and the development of widely used R packages. His work bridges theoretical statistics and applied data science, making sophisticated methods accessible to researchers across disciplines.

At IQSS, Dr. Iacus will contribute to multiple initiatives including research computing infrastructure, statistical methodology development, and collaboration with faculty and students on computationally intensive research projects. His appointment reflects IQSS's commitment to maintaining world-class expertise in quantitative methods and providing Harvard researchers with access to cutting-edge statistical and computational support. The IQSS community looks forward to the contributions Dr. Iacus will make to advancing quantitative social science research and methodology.
