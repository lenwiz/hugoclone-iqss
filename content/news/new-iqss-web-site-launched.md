The Institute for Quantitative Social Science is proud to announce the launch of a completely redesigned website, developed through an extensive cross-team collaboration that brought together expertise from across the organization. The new site reflects IQSS's evolution as a hub for quantitative research methods, data science innovation, and collaborative scholarship, providing improved access to information about programs, services, and research initiatives.

The redesigned website features enhanced navigation, making it easier for visitors to discover the breadth of resources and support available through IQSS. From data science consultation services and training opportunities to information about affiliated faculty research and major projects like Dataverse, the new site provides clearer pathways to the tools and expertise that support quantitative research across Harvard. The modern design better showcases the innovative work happening at IQSS while maintaining accessibility and usability for diverse audiences.

The collaborative development process involved contributions from technical staff, program managers, researchers, and communications specialists working together to ensure the site serves the needs of the IQSS community. The launch represents a significant milestone in IQSS's ongoing efforts to enhance communication and engagement with researchers across Harvard and beyond. The new website provides a stronger foundation for sharing news, highlighting research achievements, and connecting scholars with the resources they need to advance quantitative social science.
