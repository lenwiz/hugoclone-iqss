IQSS leadership shared an update on the Dataverse Project, reflecting on deep dives into the many areas and layers of their work. The institute has been looking for ways to maximize its impact and position itself for even greater success in research data management.

The directors discussed strategic priorities for the Dataverse platform, including expanding its global reach, improving user experience, and strengthening the community of contributors who maintain and extend the open-source software. These efforts come as research data sharing requirements from funders and journals continue to grow.

The update signals IQSS's continued commitment to the Dataverse Project as a cornerstone of its mission, emphasizing both technical excellence and community building as keys to sustainable growth in the research data infrastructure space.
