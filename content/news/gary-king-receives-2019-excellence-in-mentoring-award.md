The Society for Political Methodology named Gary King, Albert J. Weatherhead III University Professor and IQSS Director, as the 2019 recipient of the Excellence in Mentoring Award. The award recognizes sustained contributions to training the next generation of political methodologists.

King has mentored dozens of PhD students who have gone on to faculty positions at leading universities, building a network of scholars who advance quantitative methods in political science. His mentoring approach emphasizes both technical rigor and the practical skills needed to build successful academic careers.

The award reflects King's dual impact on the field—not only through his own prolific research contributions but through his investment in developing future leaders who carry forward the quantitative social science tradition that IQSS embodies.
