Kenneth A. Shepsle, IQSS affiliate and George D. Markham Professor of Government, received the 2023 William H. Riker prize in Political Science from the University of Rochester. The prize recognizes lifetime contributions to the scientific study of politics through formal theory and its applications.

Shepsle's career has been defined by groundbreaking work on legislative institutions, congressional committees, and the structure of political choice. His research on institutional design and legislative bargaining has fundamentally shaped how political scientists understand how rules and procedures affect policy outcomes.

The Riker Prize is particularly fitting given Shepsle's role in advancing the rational choice tradition in political science—the same tradition that William Riker helped establish. The award recognizes not only individual achievements but contributions to building an entire subfield of political inquiry.
