IQSS Data Science Services is excited to announce a comprehensive schedule of 12 free data science workshops for the fall semester, offering Harvard researchers valuable opportunities to develop new skills and enhance their computational research capabilities. These workshops cover a diverse range of topics essential to modern quantitative research, from programming fundamentals to advanced statistical methods and specialized tools for data analysis.

The fall workshop series is designed to serve researchers at various skill levels, from those just beginning to incorporate computational methods into their work to experienced practitioners looking to expand their toolkit. Topics span popular programming languages like R and Python, version control with Git, data visualization techniques, machine learning methods, text analysis, and more. Each workshop provides hands-on, practical instruction that participants can immediately apply to their own research projects. The workshops are taught by experienced data science consultants who understand the specific needs and challenges facing academic researchers.

These workshops are completely free for Harvard affiliates and represent one of the many ways that IQSS supports quantitative research across the university. Whether you're working in the social sciences, humanities, public health, or any field that involves data analysis, the fall workshop series offers valuable learning opportunities. Space in these popular workshops is typically limited, so interested researchers are encouraged to register early. The full schedule and registration information are available on the IQSS Data Science Services website.
