The Dataverse Project is pleased to announce that registration is now open for the 8th Annual Dataverse Community Meeting, bringing together the global network of researchers, repository administrators, developers, and data professionals who make up the vibrant Dataverse ecosystem. This year's meeting offers a valuable opportunity to connect with peers, learn about the latest platform developments, and contribute to shaping the future of research data management infrastructure.

The 2022 meeting will feature a rich program of presentations, workshops, and discussions covering diverse aspects of the Dataverse platform and research data management more broadly. Attendees can expect sessions on new software features, case studies from institutions implementing Dataverse in innovative ways, technical deep dives for developers, and strategic discussions about data curation, preservation, and open science policy. The meeting provides crucial face-to-face time for collaboration and networking that strengthens the community bonds essential to successful open-source development.

Whether you're managing an established Dataverse repository, planning a new implementation, contributing to software development, or simply interested in research data infrastructure, this meeting offers valuable content and connections. The annual gathering has become an essential event for staying current with Dataverse developments and engaging with the collaborative community that drives the project forward. Interested participants are encouraged to register early to secure their places at this important event.
