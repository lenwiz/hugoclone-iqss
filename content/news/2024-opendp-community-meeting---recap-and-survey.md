The 2024 OpenDP Community Meeting was held August 22-23 at Harvard University, once again joining forces with TPDP (Theory and Practice of Differential Privacy). The two-day event brought together differential privacy researchers, software developers, and data practitioners from academia, government, and industry.

Key highlights included demonstrations of new OpenDP Library features, panel discussions on differential privacy deployment challenges, and breakout sessions on topics such as privacy budget management and user-facing tools. Participants also engaged in collaborative problem-solving around real-world privacy scenarios.

Following the meeting, organizers circulated a survey to gather community feedback on the event format, content preferences, and priority areas for future development. The OpenDP team continues to use these gatherings as a critical touchpoint for aligning the project's technical roadmap with community needs.
