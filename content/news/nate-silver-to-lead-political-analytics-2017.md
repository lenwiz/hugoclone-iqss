The Center for American Political Studies announced the second annual Political Analytics Conference on March 31, 2017, with FiveThirtyEight founder Nate Silver as the keynote speaker. The conference, co-sponsored by IQSS, brings together academics, journalists, and practitioners working at the intersection of data science and politics.

The conference examines how quantitative methods are transforming political campaigns, journalism, and governance. Topics range from election forecasting and survey methodology to social media analytics and political communication strategies.

By hosting this conference, IQSS and CAPS create a venue where the academic rigor of university research meets the practical demands of real-world political analysis—fostering dialogue between scholars developing new methods and professionals applying them in campaigns, newsrooms, and government.
