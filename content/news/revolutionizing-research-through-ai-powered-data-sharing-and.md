TurboCurator by ICPSR is an interactive tool designed to streamline research data deposit at Dataverse, developed through intensive collaboration between institutions. The tool uses artificial intelligence to automate many of the time-consuming steps in preparing research data for sharing.

Traditionally, depositing data in a repository requires researchers to manually enter metadata, organize files, and ensure compliance with standards—a process that can take hours and discourages many from sharing their data at all. TurboCurator uses AI to suggest metadata, identify data types, and guide researchers through the curation process.

This collaboration between ICPSR and the Dataverse Project represents an important step toward making data sharing as frictionless as possible, removing barriers that prevent researchers from making their work reproducible and accessible to the broader scholarly community.
