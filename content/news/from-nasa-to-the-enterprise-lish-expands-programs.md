The Laboratory for Innovation Science at Harvard (LISH), one of IQSS's scientific programs, has experienced unprecedented growth over the past two years. Originally evolving from the NASA Tournament Lab, LISH now works with partner organizations across government, industry, and non-profit sectors on crowd-sourced problem solving.

Under its new expanded mandate, LISH conducts research on how organizations can leverage distributed intelligence, open innovation, and AI to solve complex problems. The lab's work combines rigorous experimental methods with practical applications in areas ranging from space exploration to drug discovery.

LISH's growth reflects broader trends in how scientific research is conducted—moving from individual labs to distributed networks of contributors. By studying and facilitating these new modes of collaboration, LISH contributes both practical solutions for partner organizations and fundamental insights into the science of innovation.
