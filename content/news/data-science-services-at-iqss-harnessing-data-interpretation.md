IQSS Data Science Services (DSS) provides research support to the Harvard community through consulting, collaboration, and analytical tool building. The team of PhD data science specialists assists researchers at every stage of the research process, from study design through publication.

The article profiles how academic research cycles follow systematic stages—from formulating research questions through data collection, analysis, and dissemination—and how DSS provides tailored support at each phase. Whether researchers need short-term advice on statistical methods or long-term collaborative partnerships, DSS adapts its services accordingly.

Since its founding, DSS has supported hundreds of research projects across Harvard's schools, contributing to publications in leading journals and helping researchers overcome methodological obstacles that might otherwise stall their work. The team's expertise spans statistics, machine learning, causal inference, and research software development.
