Kevin DeLuca (Yale, Harvard PhD 2023, IQSS alumnus) describes political endorsement data he collected as part of his dissertation research, exploring how newspaper endorsements relate to candidate quality and electoral outcomes.

DeLuca's research assembled a comprehensive dataset of newspaper endorsements across multiple election cycles, enabling systematic analysis of patterns that were previously only studied anecdotally. His findings contribute to understanding the role of institutional media in democratic elections.

The project demonstrates the value of IQSS's training environment, where graduate students develop both the methodological skills and the data collection persistence needed to produce original datasets that advance political science. DeLuca's subsequent position at Yale reflects the competitive academic placements that IQSS affiliates regularly achieve.
