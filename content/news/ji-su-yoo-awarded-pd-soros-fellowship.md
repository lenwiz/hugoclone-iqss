Ji Su Yoo, a Harvard alumna and IQSS Fellow, was named a 2020 recipient of the Paul & Daisy Soros Fellowship for New Americans. The prestigious fellowship supports outstanding graduate students who are immigrants or children of immigrants in the United States.

Yoo's research focuses on technology in decision-making, exploring how algorithmic systems affect fairness, accountability, and transparency in high-stakes contexts. Her work at the intersection of computer science and public policy reflects IQSS's interdisciplinary approach to studying technology's social impact.

The Soros Fellowship recognizes Yoo's potential to make significant contributions to the United States through her academic work and beyond, highlighting the caliber of researchers who develop their careers within the IQSS community.
