The Program on Survey Research (PSR), led by Associate Director Chase Harrison, helps a lot of people at Harvard and beyond with survey methodology. When COVID-19 forced a shift from face-to-face to online research, PSR became an essential resource for scholars adapting their methods.

Harrison and his team provided guidance on transitioning in-person surveys to digital formats, addressing challenges around response rates, sample representativeness, and measurement equivalence across modes. Their expertise helped researchers maintain data quality during an unprecedented disruption to social science fieldwork.

PSR's role during the pandemic highlighted the program's broader value to the Harvard research community: providing methodological expertise that enables better research design, execution, and analysis across all the social science disciplines represented at IQSS.
