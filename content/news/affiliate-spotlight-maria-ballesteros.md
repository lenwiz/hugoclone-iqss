Maria Ballesteros is a graduate student and resident affiliate at IQSS, pursuing her PhD in the Department of Government at Harvard. Her research focuses on political behavior, institutions, and methodology, with particular attention to how political systems shape citizen engagement and representation.

As a GSAS Prize Fellow, Ballesteros brings exceptional academic credentials to her work at IQSS, where she benefits from the institute's collaborative environment, research computing resources, and community of quantitative social scientists. She regularly participates in IQSS seminars and workshops.

Her affiliation with IQSS provides access to research grants, shared office space, and connections with faculty and fellow graduate students working across diverse areas of social science—resources that support her dissertation research and professional development.
