The Extraordinary Claims, Extraordinary Evidence (ECEE) program at IQSS supports Harvard faculty undertaking high-risk research that could provide novel insights by testing dominant views in social science. The program provides tailored support including data expertise, infrastructure, and funding.

ECEE recognizes that scientific progress often comes from researchers willing to challenge established consensus—but that such work faces unique obstacles in traditional funding and publication systems. By providing a confidential environment with dedicated resources, the program enables faculty to pursue ideas they might otherwise set aside.

The program's approach reflects IQSS Director Gary King's broader philosophy about scientific innovation: that extraordinary claims require extraordinary evidence, and that providing researchers with the tools to collect such evidence is one of the most valuable things an institute can do.
