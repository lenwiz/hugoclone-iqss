Harvard's Center for Geographic Analysis is pleased to announce that registration is now open for the Winter 2022 GIS Institute, a comprehensive two-week intensive training course that provides participants with foundational and advanced skills in geographic information systems and spatial analysis. This immersive program is designed for graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, faculty members, and professionals who want to incorporate spatial analysis into their research or professional practice.

The GIS Institute offers structured, hands-on instruction covering essential GIS concepts, software tools, and analytical techniques. Participants learn to work with spatial data, create compelling maps, perform sophisticated geographic analyses, and integrate GIS methods into research workflows. The intensive format allows for deep engagement with the material, progressing from fundamental concepts to advanced applications over the two-week period. Expert instructors from the Center for Geographic Analysis provide personalized guidance, helping participants overcome learning challenges and connect GIS methods to their specific research questions.

This training opportunity is particularly valuable for researchers whose work involves spatial dimensions—whether analyzing demographic patterns, environmental systems, political geography, historical change, or any phenomenon that varies across space. The skills gained during the Institute enable participants to ask and answer spatially-informed research questions with rigor and sophistication. The Winter 2022 offering provides an ideal opportunity to develop these valuable capabilities during the academic break. Interested individuals are encouraged to review the curriculum details and register early, as spaces in this popular program are limited.
