IQSS has rebranded its Peer Pre Review service as *rapid*Peer.

The service addresses frustrations with "the speed and quality of your journal peer reviews" by offering free, double-blind peer reviews for research papers, grants, or preregistration plans within 30 days. Reviewers are field-specific experts from leading institutions who provide feedback on both quantitative and qualitative research.

For Harvard researchers, *rapid*Peer delivers "quick, actionable insights that help refine your work and expedite the publication process," potentially saving months in the submission cycle. To date, the service has supported 140 scholars in maximizing their publishing success.

Researchers can visit the rapidPeer website to learn more or email rapidpeer@iq.harvard.edu for direct inquiries.
