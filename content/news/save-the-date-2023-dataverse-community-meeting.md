The Dataverse Project invites the global research data management community to save the date for the 9th Annual Dataverse Community Meeting, taking place in the historic and vibrant city of Braga, Portugal. This highly anticipated gathering brings together repository administrators, developers, data professionals, and researchers from institutions worldwide to share experiences, discuss best practices, and shape the future of the Dataverse platform.

The meeting in Braga will feature technical presentations, workshops, and collaborative sessions covering the latest developments in Dataverse software, emerging trends in research data management, and strategies for supporting open science initiatives. Attendees will have opportunities to learn from peers managing Dataverse installations across diverse institutional contexts, from small university repositories to large international collaborations. The European location makes this an especially valuable opportunity for institutions across Europe, Africa, and the Middle East to participate in the Dataverse community.

This annual gathering has become an essential event for the Dataverse ecosystem, fostering the collaborative spirit that drives the project's open-source development. Whether you're operating an established Dataverse repository, planning a new installation, or simply interested in research data management infrastructure, the meeting offers valuable networking, learning, and partnership opportunities. Detailed program information and registration details will be announced in the coming months.
