IQSS affiliate Latanya Sweeney, director of the Data Privacy Lab and Professor of Government, was appointed to join the Federal Trade Commission as Chief Technologist in January 2014. The appointment brought deep technical expertise on data privacy to the nation's primary consumer protection agency.

Sweeney's research has demonstrated how supposedly anonymous data can be re-identified, revealing fundamental vulnerabilities in how organizations handle personal information. Her work has influenced privacy regulations and corporate data practices worldwide, making her uniquely qualified for the FTC role.

The appointment exemplified how IQSS faculty translate academic research into real-world policy impact. Sweeney's FTC tenure allowed her to apply data privacy insights directly to regulatory decisions affecting millions of consumers.
