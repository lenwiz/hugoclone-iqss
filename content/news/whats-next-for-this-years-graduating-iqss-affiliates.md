The IQSS community includes affiliated graduate students across Harvard University engaged in a wide range of social science research. Each year, some graduate students depart as they complete their programs. Below are updates from graduating affiliates moving on to continue their work beyond Harvard.

## Stephanie Kestelman (PhD, Economics)

Stephanie is remaining at Harvard for a postdoctoral fellowship, working with Professor Rebecca Diamond to evaluate potential impacts of Cambridge's new zoning ordinance.

## Raphaël Raux (PhD, Economics)

Raphaël will join MIT Sloan for a 3-year postdoctoral position focused on experiments studying human-AI interaction and understanding how GenAI adoption disrupts behavior in online spaces and our relationship to technology.

## Anthony Bald (PhD, Public Policy)

This fall, Anthony will join California State University, Fullerton as an assistant professor of economics.

## Juan Dodyk (PhD, Government)

Juan will join Washington University in St. Louis as an assistant professor in political science and environmental studies.

## Lily An (PhD, Education Policy & Program Evaluation)

In August, Lily will start as an assistant professor of quantitative methodology in the Educational Policy Studies department at Georgia State University.

## Dorit Talia Stein (PhD, Population Health Sciences & Health Systems)

Over the summer, Dorit plans to visit family in San Diego, surf and present research in Indonesia, and collaborate with colleagues to disseminate research findings in Uganda. In the fall, she begins as a research fellow in the Health Systems Innovation Lab in the Department of Global Health and Population at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health.

## Shelby Carvalho (PhD, Government)

Shelby will start as a postdoctoral fellow at the King Center on Global Development at Stanford University.

## Roman Klimke (PhD, Public Policy)

After graduation, Roman will join the ifo Center in Fürth, Germany, as a postdoctoral fellow, continuing his research on social policy and labor economics.

## Serhat Yildirim (MMSc, Global Health Delivery)

Serhat will join the FXB Center for Health and Human Rights at Harvard University as a Research Associate, focusing on health and social justice, health disparities, and the intersection of healthcare and human rights.

## Emma Ebowe (PhD, Government, African & African American Studies)

Next year, Emma will join the College of William and Mary as an assistant professor in the Government and Africana Studies departments.

## Christopher Kenny (PhD, Government)

Next year, Chris will join Princeton's Data-Driven Social Sciences as a Postdoctoral Research Associate.

Congratulations to all graduating affiliates, from their colleagues at IQSS!
