Profiles of departing graduate students from the IQSS community cohort as they share plans after the academic year ends. The 2023 graduating class represents the diversity of disciplines and methodological approaches that characterize the IQSS affiliate community.

Graduating affiliates are heading to faculty positions, postdoctoral fellowships, and research roles at institutions across the country and around the world. Their research spans political science, economics, sociology, public policy, and data science—united by a commitment to rigorous quantitative methods.

The annual tradition of documenting graduates' next steps provides a snapshot of the academic job market and highlights the competitive outcomes that IQSS affiliates achieve. The institute's combination of research resources, community, and mentorship helps prepare students for success in an increasingly demanding academic landscape.
