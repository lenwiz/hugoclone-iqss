Adam Stefkovics is a visiting researcher at IQSS with a Fulbright scholarship, an assistant lecturer at Eotvos Lorand University in Budapest, and director of polling at the Szazadveg Foundation. His research focuses on survey methodology, political polling, and public opinion analysis in Central European democracies.

During his time at IQSS, Stefkovics has been working on projects related to survey design and measurement, benefiting from the institute's expertise in quantitative methods and access to collaborative research resources. His work bridges Hungarian and American academic traditions in political science and survey research.

As a Fulbright scholar, Stefkovics represents the kind of international collaboration that IQSS actively fosters through its visiting researcher and affiliate programs, bringing diverse perspectives to the study of democratic institutions and public opinion formation.
