Jacob is a resident graduate student at IQSS who is graduating this spring with a PhD in Government and Social Policy. If you see him around CGIS, be sure to wish him congratulations and good luck!

### What is the focus of your work?

"Political geography and political behavior in American politics."

### What's something you like about IQSS?

"The grad students have semi-regular dinners that are a lot of fun."

### What are you doing next after graduation?

"Spending a year at Princeton as a CSDP fellow then starting at BU as an assistant professor."
