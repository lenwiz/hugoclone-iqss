IQSS Future of Media Project fellow Heidi Legg published a Canadian Media Ownership Index detailing ownership and digital consumption patterns of Canada's top 125 news outlets. The research comes at a critical time for media industry analysis, as ownership concentration raises questions about editorial independence and information diversity.

The index provides transparency about who controls the information channels that Canadians rely on for news, revealing patterns of consolidation and the growing influence of digital platforms on traditional media business models. This data-driven approach to media analysis reflects the Future of Media Project's mission at IQSS.

By documenting ownership structures and audience metrics together, the research enables policymakers, journalists, and citizens to better understand the economic forces shaping their media environment—essential knowledge for maintaining healthy democratic discourse.
