The OpenDP Project is excited to announce that registration is now open for the 2023 OpenDP Community Meeting, marking the first in-person gathering of the differential privacy community centered around this groundbreaking open-source initiative. This event brings together researchers, developers, data practitioners, and privacy advocates who are advancing the science and practice of differential privacy through the OpenDP ecosystem.

The community meeting will feature technical presentations, hands-on workshops, and collaborative discussions about the latest developments in differential privacy tools and methodologies. Attendees will have the opportunity to learn about new features in the OpenDP Library, share implementation experiences, and contribute to the project's future direction. Whether you're already using OpenDP in production systems or exploring how differential privacy can enhance your data protection practices, this gathering offers valuable networking and learning opportunities.

Registration is open to anyone interested in differential privacy, from academic researchers and students to industry practitioners and government data stewards. The event represents a crucial milestone in building a vibrant, collaborative community around practical differential privacy tools. Interested participants are encouraged to register early, as space may be limited for this inaugural in-person meeting of the OpenDP community.
