Philip Durbin recounts a visit by five colleagues to Seoul National University for Dataverse talks and training, hosted by Professor Wonsuk Lee. The trip reflects the Dataverse Project's growing international presence and commitment to supporting installations worldwide.

The visit included technical training sessions on Dataverse administration, presentations on best practices for research data management, and discussions about how to adapt the platform for Korean research communities. Such visits help ensure that international Dataverse installations receive the support they need to serve their local research communities effectively.

The collaboration with Seoul National University exemplifies how the Dataverse Project, based at IQSS, builds relationships with institutions globally—creating a network of mutual support that strengthens research data infrastructure across borders and disciplines.
