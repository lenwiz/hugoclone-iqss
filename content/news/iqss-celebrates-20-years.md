The year 2025 marks the 20th anniversary of the Institute for Quantitative Social Science at Harvard University. The milestone was celebrated at the Center for Geographic and International Studies (CGIS) with faculty, staff, and students from across the IQSS community who work to advance research at Harvard and other institutions.

Over two decades, IQSS has developed into a hub where leading minds in data and social science collaborate on solutions to pressing societal issues. As the institute marks this milestone and reflects on its accomplishments, projects, and innovations, it is also looking ahead—including new programs and partnerships that will launch at IQSS.

The celebration acknowledged all those who have "worked across disciplinary and institutional lines" to contribute to IQSS's journey, making it "a center of intellectual curiosity, scholarly cooperation, and groundbreaking impact."
