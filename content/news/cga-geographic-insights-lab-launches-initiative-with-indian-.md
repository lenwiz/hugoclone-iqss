The CGA Geographic Insights Lab has launched a significant initiative with the Government of India, with India Policy Insights being designated as a Key Initiative of NITI Aayog, the premier policy think tank of the Indian government. This partnership represents a major collaboration between Harvard researchers and Indian policymakers to leverage geospatial data and analysis for evidence-based governance and development planning across one of the world's most populous and diverse nations.

India Policy Insights provides Indian policymakers, researchers, and citizens with accessible, data-driven insights about development indicators across India's states and districts. The platform integrates diverse data sources including government surveys, census information, and administrative records, presenting them through interactive maps and visualizations that enable users to understand geographic patterns in education, health, economic development, infrastructure, and other critical policy domains. By making complex data accessible and actionable, the initiative supports more informed policy decisions at national, state, and local levels.

The designation as a Key Initiative of NITI Aayog reflects the Indian government's recognition of the platform's value for evidence-based policymaking. The Geographic Insights Lab's work demonstrates how geographic analysis and data visualization can bridge the gap between data collection and policy impact, particularly in large, complex countries where subnational variation is substantial. The collaboration exemplifies CGA's global engagement and its capacity to apply geospatial expertise to pressing development challenges worldwide.
