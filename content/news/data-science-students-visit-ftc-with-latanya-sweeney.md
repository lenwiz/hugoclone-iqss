Professor Latanya Sweeney traveled with IQSS staff to share her students' work with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission. As director of the Data Privacy Lab at IQSS, Sweeney has long focused on the intersection of technology, privacy, and public policy.

The visit gave students a rare opportunity to present their research findings directly to federal regulators responsible for protecting consumers from unfair or deceptive practices in the digital marketplace. Projects presented addressed real-world privacy vulnerabilities in commercial technologies and proposed evidence-based policy solutions.

This engagement between Harvard researchers and the FTC exemplifies IQSS's commitment to bridging academic research and real-world impact, particularly in areas where technology creates new challenges for privacy and civil liberties.
