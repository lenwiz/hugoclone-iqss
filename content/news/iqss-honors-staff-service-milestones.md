The Institute for Quantitative Social Science held its annual recognition event celebrating staff who reached service milestones of 10 years or more during 2025. Four staff members reached the 20-year mark: Sonia Barbosa, Roy Guyton, William Horka, and Ellen Kraffmiller. Three were recognized for 15 years of service: Stephen Kraffmiller, Liz Salazar, and Len Wisniewski. Three more hit the 10-year milestone: Linda Christlieb, Sarah Duncan, and Brooke Moore.
