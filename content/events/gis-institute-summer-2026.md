## GIS Institute Summer 2026

**Date:** June 8–18, 2026

**Time:** 9:30 AM – 5:00 PM EDT

**Location:** 1737 Cambridge Street, CGIS Knafel Building, Room K350

A two-week training course in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and its application to research. Designed for Harvard graduate students, post-docs, faculty, staff, and external researchers seeking to integrate spatial thinking, incorporate GIS methods, learn spatial analysis tools, and communicate with maps. Registration fee is $100 for Harvard affiliates, $3,150 for others. Application deadline: March 22, 2026.
