## Getting Started with Spatial Analysis in R

**Date:** March 6, 2026

**Time:** 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM EST

**Location:** CGIS Knafel Building, Room SB-12

A two-hour workshop introducing spatial analysis concepts and applications. Participants will use R to perform proximity, overlay, and geostatistical operations on a variety of data and learn to interpret, export, and visualize results. Free for Harvard affiliates; $100 for others.
