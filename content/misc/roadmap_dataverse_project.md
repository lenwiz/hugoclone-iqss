</a><h2>Strategic Goals</h2><p>The Strategic Goals of the Dataverse Project are to:</p><ul><li>Grow the Dataverse community</li><li>Empower the open source community to explore and implement new Dataverse applications, tools, and services</li><li>Develop the capability to handle sensitive data and big data</li><li>Expand data and metadata features for existing and new disciplines</li><li>Expand archival and preservation features</li><li>Increase interoperability through the implementation of standards</li><li>Increase contributions from the open source development community</li><li>Improve the Dataverse user experience</li></ul><h2>Roadmap</h2><p dir="ltr">The Dataverse UI is being redesigned as a single-page application (SPA) in which internal and external Dataverse services will be delivered solely through improved APIs. This change will improve UI responsiveness and empower the Dataverse community to develop their own UIs and applications using extended Dataverse API endpoints. </p><p dir="ltr">Read more about our roadmap for the Dataverse infrastructure redesign in: <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/19pbENuYyHErEmblbFGQ47_uJpTfqVKbn9O0QftVqeeU/edit?usp=sharing">Restructuring the Dataverse UI as a Single-Page Application</a>.</p><a href="/file_url/2714"><div class="hwp-media hwp-media--full-width">

</a><p dir="ltr"><a href="/file_url/2714">View full size</a></p><h2>Current and Past Projects</h2><ul><li><a href="https://datascience.nih.gov/data-ecosystem/exploring-a-generalist-repository-for-nih-funded-data">Generalist Repository Ecosystem Initiative</a> (GREI). Multi-year grant starting in 2022 to develop collaborative approaches for data management and sharing through inclusion and enrichment of generalist repositories in the NIH data ecosystem, including the Harvard Dataverse Repository. The <a href="/news/dataverse-joins-nih-data-repository-initiative">announcement</a> has details of areas being explored.</li><li><a href="https://github.com/IQSS/dataverse-pm/issues/22">FAIR Principles for Geospatial Data</a>. Supplemental grants to expand Harvard Dataverse Repository's capacity to store and share geospatial public health data. For details, see: <a href="https://reporter.nih.gov/project-details/10594215">National Cohort Studies of Alzheimer's Disease, Related Dementias and Air Pollution</a> (Aims 1-3) and <a href="https://reporter.nih.gov/project-details/10593837">Approaches for AI/ML Readiness for Wildfire Exposures</a> (Aim 3).</li></ul><h2>Project Board</h2><p>Our <a href="https://github.com/orgs/IQSS/projects/34">project board on GitHub</a> is open for public review and comment.
