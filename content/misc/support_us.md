<h2 class="hwp-text-block__heading text-medium">
      Other Ways to Make a Contribution
    </h2>
<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p><br/>Email <strong>Elizabeth Jackson, Executive Director of IQSS</strong>, at <a href="mailto:ejackson@iq.harvard.edu">ejackson@iq.harvard.edu</a> (or send by surface mail):</p><ul><li>Purpose(s) of your donation (e.g. “for the unrestricted use of IQSS,” or “to support continued research and development of open-source data management tools at IQSS”)</li><li>Amount</li><li>Payment schedule (i.e. immediately, or <em>x</em> amount each year for <em>y</em> years)</li><li>Giving entity (e.g. Donor Advised Fund, Family Foundation, Self, etc.)</li><li>Mechanism (check, wire, stock, etc.)</li><li>Whether the gift should remain anonymous or how the gift should be acknowledged</li></ul><p>Upon receipt of your email, IQSS staff will prepare a proposed agreement with your intentions, as well as the University’s standard terms, and will revert for your signature in about a week.</p><p><em>Note: Harvard’s Federal Tax ID is ‌04-2103580</em></p></div>
