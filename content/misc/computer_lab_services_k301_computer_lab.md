<p>	The K301 Computer Lab is located on the 3rd floor of CGIS Knafel Building (1737 Cambridge Street). To get to K301, take the elevator to the third floor and go to the corner room nearest the circular staircase.</p><p>	This Lab has six Dell towers with 27" LCDs running Windows 11 and two Apple Mac Studio Max's available for users. A high-speed, black-and-white/color laser printer is located in the lab.  We also provide study tables with room for five people, including five monitors for laptops. One LG Ultrafine Display (USB-C Only), one Apple Studio Display (USB-C Only), and three 27-inch Dell monitors.</p><p>	The IQSS Computer Lab is open 24 hours a day, seven days a week.</p><p>	Special Features:</p><ul> <li>		2 Standing Desks	</li> <li>		Apple Studio Display	</li> <li>		Glorious Sunlight	</li> <li>		Study Tables with Monitors	</li></ul></div>
