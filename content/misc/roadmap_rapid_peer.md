</a></a><h2>Our winning aspiration</h2><p>Our aim is to set a new standard for the research journey across Harvard and the world by speeding up the delivery of quality, trustworthy research.</p><h2>Our playing field</h2><p>We’ll continue to support:</p><ul><li><strong>Universities:</strong> All Harvard departments, Washington University in St. Louis, and University of Notre Dame.</li><li><strong>Roles &amp; Disciplines:</strong> grad students, post-docs, visiting fellows, professors, etc from disciplines focused on empirical research</li><li><strong>Research Artifacts:</strong> e.g. grants, preregistration plans, incomplete/unfinished papers, finished papers</li></ul><h2>Our recipe for success</h2><ul><li><strong>Give researchers a competitive edge:</strong> Reduce overall time to publication by helping users go down the right path at every stage, and providing quick review turnaround &amp; quality feedback</li><li><strong>Remove apprehension of review of unpolished papers:</strong> Provide easy-to-use product/service and improve psychological safety</li><li><strong>Ensure quality, timely review:</strong> Improve reviewer incentives</li></ul><h2>Our approach to scaling</h2><ul><li><strong>Finding product-market fit:</strong> User interviewing &amp; targeted marketing on a smaller scale to ensure product-market fit</li><li><strong>Scaling promotion:</strong> Scale targeted owned, earned and paid marketing efforts</li></ul><h2>Our Roadmap</h2><a href="/sites/g/files/omnuum8171/files/harvard-iqss/files/2024-07-30_rapidpeer_roadmap_-_without_aspirational.png"><div class="hwp-media hwp-media--full-width">
