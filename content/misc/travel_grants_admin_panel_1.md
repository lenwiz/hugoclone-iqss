<p>Travel grants are available to IQSS graduate student affiliates currently enrolled in a doctoral program and are meant to support travel to facilitate presentation at a professional conference relevant to their research.</p><h2>IQSS travel grants are currently closed to applications.</h2><p> </p><ul><li>Eligibility - IQSS graduate student affiliate currently enrolled in a doctoral program</li><li>Amount - $1000 maximum awarded, only one application per fiscal year (July 1 – June 30). Grant designed to supplement costs not paid for by your home department. May be used in combination with other travel grant funds</li><li>Process - Submit travel proposal, including destination, budget with costs itemized, dates of travel, and purpose to <a href="mailto:funding@iq.harvard.edu">funding@iq.harvard.edu</a>. Proposals will be reviewed at the end of each month. Award notifications will be made at the beginning of each month, following submission.</li><li>IQSS prefers not to grant Travel Grants retroactively. Please submit your application as far in advance of the conference as you can.   </li><li>Award Restrictions - Funds are not available for job market events.</li></ul></div>
