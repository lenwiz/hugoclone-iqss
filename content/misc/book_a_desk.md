<h2>Logging in to Robin</h2><p>You should log in using your <strong>g.harvard.edu email address </strong>(non-Harvard affiliates should email <a href="mailto:robinhelp@iq.harvard.edu">robinhelp@iq.harvard.edu</a> to be manually added).</p><ul><li>Navigate to <a href="https://dashboard.robinpowered.com/iqss/login">the IQSS Robin login page</a>.</li><li>Click “Continue with Google” and log in using your g.harvard.edu credentials.</li></ul><h2>Making a Reservation</h2><p>Once you have set up your account, you can follow these <a href="https://support.robinpowered.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000403406-Get-to-know-Robin-Desks">instructions for booking a desk</a>.</p><ul><li>Please only reserve desks for the time you will be using it.</li><li>Users can book up to 90 days in advance.</li></ul></div>
</div>

<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><h2>Room Access &amp; Security</h2><p>When reserving a desk, please send an email to <a href="mailto:robinhelp@iq.harvard.edu">robinhelp@iq.harvard.edu</a> <span><strong>at least one business day in advance</strong></span> so that a key can be prepared for you. At the end of the final day of booking, please return keys to the mailbox of the IQSS Office Manager, <a href="/people/emma-loayza">Emma Loayza</a>.</p><h2>Questions?</h2><p>If you have a question or concern that is not addressed in the <a href="/file_url/2148">Robin Quick Start Guide for IQSS</a>, please email us at <a href="mailto:robinhelp@iq.harvard.edu">robinhelp@iq.harvard.edu</a>.</p></div>
