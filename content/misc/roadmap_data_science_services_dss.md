</a><h2>Our winning aspiration</h2><p>To be the premier Harvard research facilitation organization, enabling clients to produce high-quality research at optimal speed.</p><h2>Our playing field</h2><h3>We’ll continue to support:</h3><ul><li><strong>Universities:</strong> All Harvard and MIT departments</li><li><strong>Roles:</strong> Anyone doing publication research</li><li><strong>Research Outputs:</strong> Any research artifacts that involve statistics and/or programming</li><li><strong>Services:</strong> Consulting; collaboration; tools</li></ul><h2>Our recipe for success</h2><ul><li><strong>Give researchers a competitive edge:</strong> Reduce overall time to publication by helping users quickly overcome statistics- and programming-related obstacles. Help users produce solid statistical analysis and high-quality research outputs &amp; packages by providing timely, first-rate guidance and feedback.</li><li><strong>Support all stages of the research lifecycle:</strong> Support users from early research design to post-paper submission</li></ul><h2>Our approach to scaling</h2><ul><li><strong>Targeted scaling:</strong> Analytics-based targeted marketing efforts and departmental reach-out activities, towards the goals of keeping client engagement across all Harvard departments and continuing word-of-mouth growth. </li></ul><h2>Our Roadmap</h2><a href="/sites/g/files/omnuum8171/files/harvard-iqss/files/2024-07-31_dss_roadmap.png"><div class="hwp-media hwp-media--large">
