<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><h2 class="text-align-center">Transforming Social Science Research </h2><h2 class="text-align-center">Into World-Changing Companies</h2></div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="hwp-container hwp-w-full hwp-py-32 lg:hwp-py-64">
<div class="hwp-flex hwp-flex-col hwp-flex-1 hwp-overflow-hidden">
<div class="hwp-mb-16 hwp-container-px">
<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p><strong>The IQSS Startup Foundry helps Harvard faculty transform social science insights into scalable technology companies that solve global challenges.</strong></p><h3>The Untapped Opportunity</h3><p>The social sciences represent a massive, underexplored innovation pipeline. Quantitative social science methods have improved dramatically, and analytical capabilities have exploded, yet the commercial world remains largely untapped. Most researchers don't realize how their insights could become transformative companies. This creates unprecedented opportunities for academics and the business world.</p><p>Bringing together business minds and academic discoveries can create new ventures that make significant contributions to various sectors, including healthcare, education, workplace productivity, and media. This collaboration supercharges knowledge creation and economies. Academia gains real-world datasets, resources, and expertise that accelerate research. Industry gains new companies with defensible competitive advantages rooted in scientific discovery.</p><p>The result: substantial public good and commercial value.</p><h3>Creating New Ventures</h3><p>Our mission is to foster these collaborations through building an ecosystem and a set of venture creation resources to develop companies that create significant impact on the world. The IQSS Startup Foundry is where it begins. </p></div>
