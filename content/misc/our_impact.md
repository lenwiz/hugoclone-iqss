<h3 class="fifty-fifty-card__title">Investigating Flight Attendants' Safety
    </h3>
</div>
<div class="fifty-fifty-card__text-col fifty-fifty-card__text-col-2">
<div class="fifty-fifty-card__description hwp-text-block"><p>In the longest-running longitudinal study of its kind, a study by Dr. Eileen McNeely, executive director of <a href="https://shine.sph.harvard.edu/">SHINE at IQSS</a>, collected data on uniforms from major US airlines. Laboratory tests found harmful substances on a manufacturer's uniforms, and analysis showed a clear connection between the introduction of these uniforms and reports of debilitating medical issues such as respiratory problems and thyroid disease. These findings provided evidence in a class action lawsuit holding the uniform manufacturer accountable for the resulting health effects.</p></div>

<a class="page-card__link hwp-order-2" href="/news/dataverse-publishes-houghton-librarys-slavery-abolition-emancipation-and-freedom"> <h3 class="page-card__heading hwp-order-2">Digitizing History for the Future of Data Research</h3>
</a>
<div class="page-card__description hwp-order-3 hwp-text-block"><p>In the first major endeavor to represent a significant Harvard Library collection as data, the IQSS Dataverse Project collaborated with the Houghton Library to create the Slavery, Abolition, Emancipation, and Freedom (SAEF) Data Collection, an open access research dataset of more than 1200 items relating to African American history from the Early Republic through Reconstruction.</p></div>

<a class="page-card__link hwp-order-2" href="/news/twitter-sentiment-geographical-index-sifts-through-social-media-posts-look"> <h3 class="page-card__heading hwp-order-2">Measuring Population Well-Being with Social Media</h3>
</a>
<div class="page-card__description hwp-order-3 hwp-text-block"><p>Can we derive any useful information from social media posts about movies, food, or seemingly nothing in particular? By creating the Twitter Sentiment Geographical Index (TGSI), Devika Kakkar's team at the Center for Geographical Analysis collaborated with other researchers to study a range of topics such as pollution, climate change, and emotional well-being.</p></div>

<h3 class="fifty-fifty-card__title">Our Community
    </h3>

<div class="fifty-fifty-card__text-col fifty-fifty-card__text-col-2">
<div class="fifty-fifty-card__description hwp-text-block"><p><span>IQSS is comprised of faculty and students representing nearly every school within Harvard, plus visiting scholars, fellows, and postdocs from institutions across the globe. These scholars are supported by a staff of more than 50 dedicated IT professionals, administrators, and support personnel. The strength of our community is derived from our philosophy of collaboration: the more information we share, the better the social science research and the more likely it is that we will be able to fix some of the world’s most complex social problems. Join us in our efforts!</span></p></div>
