<p>Harvard graduate students currently enrolled in a docotoral program and affiliated with IQSS are eligible to apply for funding to support dissertation research-related costs, including but not limited to:</p><ul><li>Survey development</li><li>Crowdsourcing, human subjects and payments</li><li>Supplies</li><li>Technical services</li><li>Printing and publication costs</li><li>International travel</li><li>Research assistance for specialized tasks</li></ul><p>These funds may supplement other grant programs. Please note that funds from this grant are not meant to be used for non-specialized research assistance or as a personal stipend.</p><p>Harvard graduate student affiliates who receive research grants are expected to be involved in the IQSS community. There is also an expectation that, when ready, grant recipients will submit a one- to three-page narrative report describing their research, which may be featured on the IQSS website.</p><h2>Application Requirements</h2><ul><li>Eligibility - IQSS Harvard graduate student affiliates who are currently enrolled in a doctoral program and are not graduating this year</li><li>Amount - $3,000 maximum</li><li>Deadline - <span><strong>Currently on hold</strong></span></li><li>Notification of Award - April; award distribution in early June.</li><li>Process - Submit a completed IQSS Graduate Student Funding Application via <a href="https://carat.fas.harvard.edu/applicant/newApplication?applicationPurposeId=4&amp;fundingSourceId=7265">CARAT</a>. Applicants will need to submit an application form, detailed budget, and letter of recommendation from a faculty member.</li></ul></div>
