<p>Our Computer Training Room is for instructor-led events that require students to access a computer and follow the instructor. The room (<strong>SB12</strong>) is located in the Sub-basement Level of the CGIS Knafel building and is only open during requested reservation times.</p><p>The room has <strong>twenty-two</strong> student workstations and <strong>one</strong> instructor machine, with the ability to add<strong> </strong>additional workstations for single week events with 30 days prior notice. A ceiling-mounted projector is connected to the instructor machine by default, but a hookup to another computer, such as a laptop, is also available. The room has several whiteboards and additional tables for handouts. </p><p>The workstations are OptiPlex Micro Plus 7010 running Windows 11.</p><p>If participants need to do follow-up work in our main lab, they can sign up for lab access <a data-entity-substitution="canonical" data-entity-type="node" data-entity-uuid="50638aef-23a7-4461-b951-1cd6546be141" href="/contact-labs" title="Access Request Form">here</a>. If your event includes those who are not affiliated with Harvard, we also provide guest accounts with prior notice. </p><p>The room is available to reserve Monday - Thursday from 9 AM to 5 PM and Fridays from 9 AM to 5PM. </p><p><span>The Training Classroom is primarily intended for Harvard affiliates. If a class includes non-affiliated attendees, guest accounts must be requested at least one month in advance of the first session. </span><br/><br/><span>Please submit the form below to make your reservation requests at least two weeks in advance. Please be aware that semester-long reservations should be made at the end of the prior semester to ensure availability.</span></p></div>
