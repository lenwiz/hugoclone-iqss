<p>IQSS welcomes requests from scholars who participate in the life of the Institute to become affiliates. Opportunities to participate in the life of the Institute include attending seminars, giving talks to the IQSS commmunity, working with IQSS graduate student affiliates, co-authoring or otherwise collaborating with IQSS affiliates, serving on committees, reviewing scholarly grant applications, attenting events, and sharing in a pot of coffee in the IQSS "Quantina" common space. Below is a list of affiliations and requirements. Affiliation also allows graduate students and Harvard Faculty to apply for funding or access IQSS-based services, such as Sponsored Research Services. If you have further questions, please contact <a href="mailto:affiliation@iq.harvard.edu">affiliation@iq.harvard.edu</a>.</p></div>

<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p> <strong>Harvard Faculty Affiliates</strong> are able to apply for funding through IQSS's <a href="https://urs.iq.harvard.edu/">Undergraduate Research Scholars Program (URS)</a>, and may request the services of the <a href="/sponsored-research-services">IQSS Sponsored Team</a> when applying for sponsored research grants. They are also placed on an email list inviting them to community activities, talks, and events.  <em>Please note that faculty affiliates may be called upon to participate in reviews of scholarly grant applications.</em></p><p>	Requirements:</p><ul> <li>		Faculty who participate in the life of the Institute, broadly defined as attending seminars, advising or otherwise working with IQSS graduate student affiliates, giving talks to the IQSS community, co-authoring papers, or otherwise collaborating with other IQSS affiliates are eligible to be listed as IQSS affiliates. If you are a Harvard faculty member who fits these criteria or otherwise believe you contribute to IQSS’s mission, please send an email identifying yourself, your department, and your research interest(s) to <a href="mailto:affiliation@iq.harvard.edu">affiliation@iq.harvard.edu</a>.	</li></ul></div>

<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	The appointment of <strong>Associate</strong> allows for an unpaid affiliation with the Faculty of Arts and Sciences. It is designed for an individual whose credentials and interests align with a specific institutional objective, and it permits the appointee to participate in certain activities of the unit conferring the appointment.</p><p>	Send the following required documents to <a href="mailto:bobrien@iq.harvard.edu">Bonnie O’Brien</a>, Director of HR:</p><ul> <li>		Request for affiliation written by and sent directly from an IQSS-affiliated Harvard faculty member;	</li> <li>		Current CV;	</li> <li>		Research statement indicating what project or collaborations the Associate will be working on while at Harvard; and	</li> <li>		Description of the Associate’s current full-time employment outside of Harvard.	</li></ul><p>	An Associate appointment may not exceed 12 months. <strong>Reappointments </strong>require the submission of a report delineating activities undertaken during the previous appointment term, a research statement outlining the project to be undertaken during the new appointment term, and an explanation of how the forthcoming project aligns with the mission of IQSS.</p></div>
