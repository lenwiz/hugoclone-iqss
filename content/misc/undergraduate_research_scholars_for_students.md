<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><p>	Harvard undergraduates can participate in the research mission of the University by working as paid research assistants to IQSS Faculty Affiliates. Faculty funded by the URS program come from a wide array of schools and disciplines at Harvard. There are two ways you can join our program:</p><p>	1.  Check out our <a href="/undergraduate-research-scholars/research-opportunities">current job postings</a> for projects in need of research assistants.</p><p> <strong>-OR-</strong></p><p>	2.  Reach out to a faculty member you would like to work for as a research assistant, and talk to them about <a href="/undergraduate-research-scholars/how-to-apply">applying</a> for a URS grant to hire you.</p></div>
