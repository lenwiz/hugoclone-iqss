<p>Whether you are interested in learning new software skills or broadening your knowledge on social science-related topics, we are here to help you learn.</p><p>Here are some opportunities to get started.</p><h2>Workshops hosted at IQSS in collaboration with other departments</h2><ul><li><a href="http://projects.iq.harvard.edu/applied.stats.workshop-gov3009">Applied Statistics Workshop</a> - a weekly, cross-disciplinary tour of Harvard's statistical innovations and applications</li><li><a href="https://projects.iq.harvard.edu/techscience/news-events">Tech Science Seminars</a> - weekly series on Technology Science from expert researchers, public interest groups, and others on the social impact of technology and its unforeseen consequences.</li><li><a href="/program-political-economy">Alberto Alesina Seminar on Political Economy</a>  - a seminar series on formal and quantitative political research that integrates the study of economics and politics.</li><li><a href="https://projects.iq.harvard.edu/pol_psych/about-wgpp">Working Group in Political Psychology</a> - a semimonthly interdisciplinary forum for psychological and empirical research that examines the microfoundations of citizen and elite behavior</li><li><a href="https://wcfia.harvard.edu/seminars/political_economy_workshop">Research Workshop in Political Economy</a>  - examines how institutions affect political and economic outcomes. (Note: this is a closed workshop.)</li></ul><h2>Additional Trainings and Workshops:</h2></div>

<div class="hwp-text-block field field--name-field-hwp-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden"><h2>	Workshops provided by the Center for Geographic Analysis (CGA)</h2><p>	The CGA offers opportunities to learn GIS-related skills both on campus and online. In addition to Harvard courses that students can take for credit, non-credit opportunities include:</p><ul> <li>		Self-guided video tutorial series	</li> <li>		GIS Institute (two-week training institute held every semester. Research proposal required)	</li> <li>		Cartography Workshop (One day workshop held every SPRING semester)	</li> <li>		GIS for the Humanities and Social Sciences (One day workshop held every FALL semester)	</li> <li>		Basic Introduction to GIS (Two-hour workshop held every semester)	</li></ul><p>	You can find a full list and details at the CGA website: <a href="https://gis.harvard.edu/training">gis.harvard.edu/training</a></p></div>
