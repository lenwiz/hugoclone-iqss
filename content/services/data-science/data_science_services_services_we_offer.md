Services We Offer

The IQSS Data Science Services (DSS) team supports researchers throughout the Harvard and MIT communities by helping them navigate challenges at every stage of the publication research process. Our team includes five consultants: Joshua Cetron, Noah Greifer, Jinjie Liu, Steven Worthington, and Dan Yuan.
