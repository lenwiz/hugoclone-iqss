IQSS Computer Labs

Who can Access?

We host multiple state-of-the-art computing labs for faculty, visiting scholars, post-docs, students, and staff. The IQSS Computer Labs use University active directory accounts that must be activated for use in our labs. New patrons can easily "sign" up for lab access by filling out our access request form.
