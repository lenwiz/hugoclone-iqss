Hardware Support

We are proud to support a variety of hardware, including desktop computers, laptops, Harvard VOIP phones, printers, scanners, and a variety of smart phones and tablets.	If you experience computing problems that you suspect are hardware related, please contact us. A support technician can assist you with hardware troubleshooting, in-warranty support requests, or hardware replacement purchasing if needed.
