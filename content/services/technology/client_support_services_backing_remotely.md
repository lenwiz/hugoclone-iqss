Backing Up Remotely

Remotely backing up your data involves copying your important files to a location that is not attached directly to your computer. Generally, this means copying your data over a network to a location on a file server. The University provides many options including OneDrive, g. harvard, Sharepoint, departmental network shares and CrashPlanProE.
