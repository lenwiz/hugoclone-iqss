Web Technologies

We recognize that the web is an increasingly important communication medium for the departments and research centers at CGIS. HUIT currently offers a variety of web-based services that include websites, mailing lists, and event management systems. Each of these systems has proven valuable for members of the CGIS community.	We support and assit with these technologies and others managed by HUIT.
