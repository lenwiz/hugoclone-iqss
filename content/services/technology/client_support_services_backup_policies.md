Backup Policies

Except where otherwise stated, HMDC-provided storage services are backed up by default. The schedule and retention policy varies depending on the aplication. Generally, back ups are retained for 3 months. The highest chance of success for recovering a file occurs if it exists on disk for 24 hours and HMDC is notified as soon as possible when corruption or deletion occurs.
