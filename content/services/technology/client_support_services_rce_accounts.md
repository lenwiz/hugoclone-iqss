RCE Accounts

When you request an RCE account, username will be the University NetID.	RCE account passwords must conform to the following restrictions: 		    At least eight characters in length	 		    Not based on any common dictionary word	 		    No spaces	 		    Contains at least two numbers	 		    Contains at least two special (nonalphanumeric) characters		Please contact us for more information.
