Mapping (Connecting To) a Departmental Network Drive

Windows 11:1) Click on the folder on the bottom bar2) Click This PC3) Click on the Computer tab4) Click Map Network Drive5) Enter the path to your department share. This may be on Dss10 or fas-deptsFor example, \\fas-depts. ad. fas. harvard. edu\cgis\your_department_name or \\dss10. ad. fas. harvard.
