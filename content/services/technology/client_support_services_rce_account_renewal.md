RCE Account Renewal

As required by Harvard security policy, all RCE accounts have a renewal date. Renewal dates are selected based on the estimated end times of users' projects. As a general rule accounts are up for renewal at least once every year. When you see an account renewal message, please respond to it. If you fail to respond, your account will be disabled.
