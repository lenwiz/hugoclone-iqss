Tech Disposal

Whenever a system, storage device, or storage procedure is disposed of, assigned to another client, or re-purposed, it must be cleaned to eliminate Harvard confidential information (HCI) data. Note: Deleting your files and or reformatting the hard drive may not be sufficient for this process.
