General Usage Policies

Information stored on a computer system or sent electronically over a network is the property of the individual who created it. Systems administrators, however, may gain access to users' data or programs when it is necessary to maintain or prevent damage to systems or to ensure compliance with other University rules. We train our systems administrators with care.
