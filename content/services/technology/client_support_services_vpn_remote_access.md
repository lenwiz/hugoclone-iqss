VPN Remote Access

To access your network space while off campus:Install and configure Okta app on your smart device if you haven't done so already. Details can be found at https://key. harvard. eduInstall Harvard's VPN Client: You can obtain this software from http://vpn. harvard. eduLog in with your Harvard username and Key and additional code from the Okta app.
