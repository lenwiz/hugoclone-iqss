Research Resources

IQSS offers access for social scientists to technological resources, and we're ready to help you learn how to use them so you can focus on your research.

rapidPeer

rapidPeer is an innovative peer pre-review service—similar to peer review for a scholarly journal, but faster, more complete, and constructive. Reviewers read the full paper and offer constructive suggestions throughout.

Extraordinary Claims, Extraordinary Evidence

The ECEE program empowers social science research that boldly tests dominant positions of the scientific community. Awardees get enhanced support from IQSS infrastructure and personnel.

Data Science Services

Our team of PhD data science specialists provides research support to the Harvard community.

Training and Workshops

Offers opportunities to learn new software skills or broaden knowledge on social science topics.

Client Support Services

Local tech support for the CGIS complex, assisting with software, hardware, computers, smartphones, tablets, and printers.

Computer Labs

IQSS hosts multiple state-of-the-art public computing labs in collaborative open-space areas for faculty, scholars, post-docs, students, and staff.

Sponsored Research Services

The Sponsored Research Administration Team supports social scientists throughout the lifecycle of a sponsored project.

Research Computing

Researchers access the Research Computing Environment (RCE) for social science computing and analysis—a centralized place for data storage and collaborative analysis.

Undergraduate Research Scholars Program

Opportunity for Harvard College students to gain first-hand research experience working with faculty across the University.
