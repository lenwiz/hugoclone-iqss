Data Science Services

IQSS Data Science Services is pleased to assist researchers in the Harvard community working on publication research in overcoming obstacles at every stage of the research process.

Advisory Services

Consulting

We provide short-term support (up to 3 hours per publication) in the form of advice.

Learn more

 

Collaboration

We provide extended support over the lifecycle of a research project by embedding a data science specialist in your research team. 

Learn more

Analytical Tool Building

We can develop R code to implement your new statistical method or to automate part of your analysis pipeline.

Learn more
