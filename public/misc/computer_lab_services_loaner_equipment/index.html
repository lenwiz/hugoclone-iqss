Item Checkout

We have a variety of items that are available for daily checkout to our patrons by stopping by lab help desk in the Concourse Lab and leaving an active ID from Harvard or MIT (Sorry, no library cards). Items must be returned 30 minutes before the help desk closes, which can vary from day to day due to staffing issues. IDs can only be returned to the ID owner.
