Travel Grants

Travel grants are available to IQSS graduate student affiliates currently enrolled in a doctoral program and are meant to support travel to facilitate presentation at a professional conference relevant to their research.

IQSS travel grants are currently closed to applications.

 

  • Eligibility - IQSS graduate student affiliate currently enrolled in a doctoral program
  • Amount - $1000 maximum awarded, only one application per fiscal year (July 1 – June 30). Grant designed to supplement costs not paid for by your home department. May be used in combination with other travel grant funds
  • Process - Submit travel proposal, including destination, budget with costs itemized, dates of travel, and purpose to funding@iq.harvard.edu. Proposals will be reviewed at the end of each month. Award notifications will be made at the beginning of each month, following submission.
  • IQSS prefers not to grant Travel Grants retroactively. Please submit your application as far in advance of the conference as you can.  
  • Award Restrictions - Funds are not available for job market events.
