Computer Training Room

Our Computer Training Room is for instructor-led events that require students to access a computer and follow the instructor. The room (SB12) is located in the Sub-basement Level of the CGIS Knafel building and is only open during requested reservation times.

The room has twenty-two student workstations and one instructor machine, with the ability to add additional workstations for single week events with 30 days prior notice. A ceiling-mounted projector is connected to the instructor machine by default, but a hookup to another computer, such as a laptop, is also available. The room has several whiteboards and additional tables for handouts. 

The workstations are OptiPlex Micro Plus 7010 running Windows 11.

If participants need to do follow-up work in our main lab, they can sign up for lab access here. If your event includes those who are not affiliated with Harvard, we also provide guest accounts with prior notice. 

The room is available to reserve Monday - Thursday from 9 AM to 5 PM and Fridays from 9 AM to 5PM. 

The Training Classroom is primarily intended for Harvard affiliates. If a class includes non-affiliated attendees, guest accounts must be requested at least one month in advance of the first session. 

Please submit the form below to make your reservation requests at least two weeks in advance. Please be aware that semester-long reservations should be made at the end of the prior semester to ensure availability.
