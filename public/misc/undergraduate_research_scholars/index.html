Undergraduate Research Scholars

The IQSS Undergraduate Research Scholars (URS) program is an opportunity for Harvard College students to gain first-hand research experience working with faculty members all across the University.

Program Benefits

More About the Program chevron_right

For Faculty

Get funding to hire Undergraduate Research Scholars as research assistants for your project.

For Students

Gain valuable experience as a paid research assistant on new and exciting research projects led by Harvard faculty.
