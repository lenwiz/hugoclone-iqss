Other Ways to Make a Contribution


Email Elizabeth Jackson, Executive Director of IQSS, at ejackson@iq.harvard.edu (or send by surface mail):

  • Purpose(s) of your donation (e.g. “for the unrestricted use of IQSS,” or “to support continued research and development of open-source data management tools at IQSS”)
  • Amount
  • Payment schedule (i.e. immediately, or x amount each year for y years)
  • Giving entity (e.g. Donor Advised Fund, Family Foundation, Self, etc.)
  • Mechanism (check, wire, stock, etc.)
  • Whether the gift should remain anonymous or how the gift should be acknowledged

Upon receipt of your email, IQSS staff will prepare a proposed agreement with your intentions, as well as the University’s standard terms, and will revert for your signature in about a week.
