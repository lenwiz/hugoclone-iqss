rapidPeer

rapidPeer is a free service offering double-blind peer reviews of research papers, grants, or preregistration plans in 30 days by field-specific experts at leading institutions

 

Why rapidPeer?

Boost your publication odds

Get a professional-quality review without using a journal submission

Accelerate your publishing timeline

Catch flaws before they do; submit with peace of mind and get published faster

Save time and money

Get early-stage feedback and head in the right direction from the start.

How Does It Work?

Submit your research

Select potential reviewers

Quality peer review delivered in 30 days

Revise and submit for publication
